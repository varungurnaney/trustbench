# Helix Software — Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** March 1, 2025
**Version:** 3.2

---

## 1. About This Policy

Helix Software GmbH ("Helix," "we," "us") provides this Privacy Policy to inform you about how we collect, process, and protect your personal information when you use our project management platform ("Helix Platform"), visit our website (www.helixsoftware.eu), or interact with our services.

Helix Software is headquartered in Berlin, Germany and operates under the jurisdiction of the European Union's General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

## 2. Information We Collect

We collect and process the following categories of personal information:

**Information you provide directly:**
- Account registration data: full name, email address, company name, job title
- Profile information: profile photo, department, timezone preference
- Communication data: messages sent through support channels, feedback submissions
- Payment information: billing address, VAT number (payment processing handled by Stripe)

**Information collected automatically:**
- Technical data: IP address, browser type and version, operating system
- Authentication data: login timestamps, session identifiers, MFA verification events
- Platform interaction data: features accessed, project activity logs, notification preferences

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We process your personal information for the following purposes:

- **Service Delivery:** Providing, maintaining, and improving the Helix Platform
- **Account Management:** Managing your subscription, authentication, and billing
- **Support:** Responding to support requests and providing technical assistance
- **Security:** Detecting and preventing unauthorized access, fraud, and abuse
- **Compliance:** Meeting legal and regulatory obligations
- **Product Improvement:** Analyzing aggregated usage patterns to enhance platform features

All processing activities are conducted under one of the following legal bases: consent, contractual necessity, legitimate interest, or legal obligation, as defined under GDPR Article 6(1).

## 4. Data Sharing

Helix Software does not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their own purposes. Your data remains exclusively within the Helix Software environment.

We may disclose personal information only in the following limited circumstances:
- When required by law, regulation, or valid legal process
- To protect the rights, property, or safety of Helix Software, our users, or the public
- In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (with prior notice to affected users)

We do not use your data for advertising purposes or share it with advertising networks.

## 5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active, plus a period of 24 months after account closure to comply with legal and regulatory requirements. After this retention period, data is permanently deleted using industry-standard secure deletion methods.

Backup copies are retained for an additional 90 days following the primary data deletion to allow for disaster recovery, after which they are also permanently destroyed.

## 6. Data Location

All personal data is processed and stored within the European Economic Area. Our primary infrastructure is hosted in AWS data centers in Frankfurt, Germany (eu-central-1) and Dublin, Ireland (eu-west-1).

We do not transfer personal data outside the EEA.

## 7. Your Rights Under GDPR

As a data subject, you have the following rights:

- **Access (Art. 15):** Request a copy of your personal data
- **Rectification (Art. 16):** Request correction of inaccurate data
- **Erasure (Art. 17):** Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
- **Restriction (Art. 18):** Request restriction of processing
- **Portability (Art. 20):** Receive your data in a machine-readable format
- **Objection (Art. 21):** Object to processing based on legitimate interest
- **Withdraw Consent (Art. 7):** Withdraw consent at any time for consent-based processing

To exercise these rights, contact our Data Protection Officer at dpo@helixsoftware.eu.

We will notify registered users via email of any material changes to this policy at least 30 days before they take effect. The current version is always available at www.helixsoftware.eu/privacy.
