## 1. Purpose

This policy establishes requirements for personnel security at Cascade Financial Technologies to ensure all individuals with access to company systems and data meet security competency standards in alignment with organizational objectives.

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to all full-time employees, part-time employees, interns, and third-party contractors who access Cascade Financial Technologies information systems, data, or facilities.

## 3. Pre-Employment Screening

### 3.1 Background Checks

All candidates must successfully complete a background check prior to being granted access to any Cascade Financial Technologies information systems. Background checks are conducted by Sterling Verification Services and include:

- Criminal history (7-year lookback)
- Employment verification (5 years)
- Education verification
- Credit check (for positions with financial responsibilities)

Background check results must be received and reviewed by HR before system access is provisioned.

### 3.2 Identity Verification

Government-issued photo identification must be verified on the first day of employment.

## 4. Security Awareness Training

### 4.1 Initial Training

All new employees must complete the KnowBe4 Security Awareness Training module within their first 14 days of employment. Training completion is tracked in the KnowBe4 platform and reported to HR weekly.

### 4.2 Annual Refresher Training

All employees and contractors must complete annual security awareness refresher training by December 31 of each calendar year. The annual training cycle opens October 1 and closes December 31.

### 4.3 Training Topics

Annual training covers phishing awareness, data handling, incident reporting, password hygiene, social engineering, and regulatory requirements specific to financial services.

### 4.4 Non-Completion Consequences

Employees who do not complete annual training by the December 31 deadline will have their system access suspended on January 15 of the following year until training is completed.

## 5. Acceptable Use Policy

### 5.1 AUP Acknowledgment

All employees and contractors must sign the Acceptable Use Policy before receiving system access credentials. Signed AUPs are stored in BambooHR.

## 6. Role Changes

### 6.1 Access Review

When an employee changes roles, the departing manager must submit an access review request within 5 business days. The IT Security team reviews and adjusts access within 10 business days of the request.

## 7. Termination

### 7.1 Access Revocation

All access must be revoked within 4 hours of termination notification.
