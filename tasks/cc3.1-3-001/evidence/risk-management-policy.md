## 1. Purpose

This policy establishes the risk management framework for Datastream Analytics Inc. to identify, assess, treat, and monitor risks in alignment with SOC 2 Trust Services Criteria CC3.1.

## 2. Risk Register

All identified risks must be documented in the risk register with:
- Risk ID, description, and category
- Likelihood and impact ratings
- Risk classification
- Treatment strategy
- Assigned controls (mapped from control catalog)
- Risk owner and review dates

## 3. Control Mapping

### 3.1 Requirement

Each risk with a treatment strategy of "Mitigate" must have at least one control mapped from the organization's control catalog. The control mapping must:
- Identify specific controls that address the risk
- Document the control's effectiveness (Effective / Partially Effective / Ineffective)
- Be reviewed when the risk rating changes

### 3.2 Control Catalog

The organization maintains a control catalog in the GRC tool. Controls are mapped to risks to demonstrate that identified risks have appropriate mitigations in place.

## 4. Risk Assessment

Annual comprehensive assessment in Q1. Quarterly risk register reviews by the SGC.

## 5. Risk Rating

5x5 likelihood-impact matrix. Classifications: Low (1-4), Medium (5-9), High (10-15), Critical (16-25).

## 6. Risk Treatment

- Mitigate: Implement controls (requires control mapping per Section 3)
- Transfer: Insurance or contract
- Accept: CISO approval for High; Board for Critical
- Avoid: Eliminate the activity
