# Physical Security Policy

**Document ID:** POL-PS-015
**Version:** 3.4
**Effective Date:** May 1, 2025
**Owner:** Security and Facilities Management
**Classification:** Internal

---

## 1. Purpose

This policy establishes Solaris Tech Group's requirements for physical access controls to protect company facilities, equipment, data, and personnel from unauthorized access, theft, damage, and environmental hazards.

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to all Solaris Tech Group facilities, including the headquarters at 3100 Innovation Court, Boulder, CO, and the satellite office at 1500 Pearl Street, Suite 400, Denver, CO.

## 3. Access Control System

### 3.1 System Overview

Solaris Tech Group uses the Lenel OnGuard access control platform with HID iCLASS SEOS readers deployed at all controlled entry points. The system enforces zone-based access restrictions and logs all badge events (granted and denied).

### 3.2 Security Zones

| Zone | Classification | Access Requirement |
|------|---------------|-------------------|
| Public | Open | No badge required |
| General Office | Controlled | Valid employee or authorized contractor badge |
| Restricted | High Security | Badge with Restricted authorization (Infrastructure Director approval) |
| Data Center | Critical | Badge with Data Center authorization (CISO approval) + biometric |

## 4. Badge Lifecycle Management

### 4.1 Employee Badge Issuance

Badges are provisioned by the Facilities team within 1 business day of the employee's start date. Access levels are assigned per the Access Authorization Request (AAR) approved by the hiring manager.

### 4.2 Badge Types

| Type | Prefix | Visual Identifier |
|------|--------|-------------------|
| Employee | B- | Blue badge with photo |
| Contractor | CTR- | Yellow badge with CONTRACTOR label |
| Visitor | VIS- | Red badge, no photo |

### 4.3 Access Level Modifications

Changes to access levels require a new AAR approved by the employee's current manager. Restricted and Data Center zone changes require Infrastructure Director or CISO approval respectively.

### 4.4 Quarterly Access Reviews

The Security Manager conducts quarterly reviews of all active badges. Badges for employees or contractors who no longer require access are deactivated within 5 business days of the review.

### 4.5 Employee Termination

Upon employee separation, HR notifies Facilities of the termination. The badge must be deactivated on the termination effective date, prior to the employee's departure from the building. The badge is physically collected during the exit interview.

### 4.6 Contractor Badge Activation

Contractor badges shall be activated on or after the contract start date, not before. The sponsoring Solaris employee must submit a Contractor Access Request (CAR) with the approved work order, contract dates, and required access zones.

### 4.7 Contractor Badge Deactivation

Contractor badges must be deactivated within 1 business day of the contract end date. The Facilities Coordinator validates contractor badge expiration dates weekly.

## 5. Surveillance

### 5.1 CCTV

IP cameras (Axis P3245-V) are deployed at all entry points, hallways, server rooms, and parking areas. Footage is retained for 90 days on the Milestone XProtect VMS.

### 5.2 Monitoring

Live monitoring during business hours by reception. After-hours motion-detection alerts to on-call Security.

## 6. Visitor Management

### 6.1 Procedures

All visitors must be pre-registered, present photo ID, and be escorted at all times. Visitors are restricted to the General Office zone.

## 7. After-Hours Access

### 7.1 Business Hours

Monday through Friday, 7:00 AM to 7:00 PM Mountain Time.

### 7.2 After-Hours Approval

Access outside business hours requires an approved After-Hours Access Form submitted by the employee's manager to the Security Manager.

### 7.3 On-Call Exceptions

Documented on-call rotations approved by the Infrastructure Director grant automatic after-hours access. Schedules must be submitted monthly.

## 8. Environmental Controls

### 8.1 Monitoring

Server rooms have temperature and humidity sensors reporting to Schneider Electric EcoStruxure IT. Alert thresholds: temperature > 77°F, humidity > 55%.
