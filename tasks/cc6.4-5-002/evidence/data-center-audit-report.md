## 1. Purpose

Annual physical security audit of Citadel Colocation as required by Prism Analytics Physical Security Policy POL-PS-025, Section 8.2. This audit independently verifies that the colocation provider's physical security controls meet Prism's requirements and are operating effectively.

## 2. Scope

Physical inspection of Citadel Colocation's Fremont facility with focus on:
- Perimeter and building access controls
- Prism cabinet security (CAB-F2-310, CAB-F2-311)
- CCTV coverage of Prism cabinet row
- Environmental controls
- Authorized access list verification

## 6. Minor Observations

- Citadel security guard shift changeover takes approximately 10 minutes with both guards overlapping — acceptable practice.
- Cabinet row F2 lighting could be improved for better CCTV image quality — recommendation, not a finding.

## 7. Provider SOC 2 Report

Citadel Colocation's SOC 2 Type II report (issued by EY, covering June 2023 – May 2024) reviewed. No exceptions noted. Citadel has issued a SOC 2 bridge letter covering the period June 2024 – November 2024, pending the next full report. NOTE: Per Policy Section 8.2, the provider's SOC 2 report does not replace Prism's independent audit, which verifies Prism-specific controls (access list accuracy, cabinet locks, dedicated CCTV coverage).
