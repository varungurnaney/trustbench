## 1. Purpose

This policy defines personnel security requirements at Helix Cloud Solutions to ensure that all individuals with access to company systems meet security competency and trustworthiness standards in support of organizational objectives.

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to all employees, contractors, interns, and temporary workers at Helix Cloud Solutions.

## 3. Pre-Employment Screening

### 3.1 Background Check Requirements

Background checks are required for all individuals prior to granting access to Helix Cloud Solutions information systems. Requirements vary by role sensitivity:

| Role Classification | Background Check Level | Completion Requirement |
|---------------------|----------------------|----------------------|
| Sensitive (production system access, financial data, PII) | Comprehensive — criminal, credit, education, employment | Must be completed before access is provisioned |
| Non-Sensitive (general corporate access only, no production/PII) | Basic — identity and employment verification only | Must be completed within 30 days of start date |

### 3.2 Background Check Provider

All background checks are conducted through Checkr, Inc. Results are delivered to the HR Operations inbox and adjudicated by the HR Compliance Specialist.

### 3.3 Pending Background Checks

If a background check is pending beyond 60 days from initiation, the HR Compliance Specialist must escalate to the CHRO for a determination on continued access. Pending checks beyond 90 days require a documented risk assessment.

## 4. Security Awareness Training

### 4.1 Initial Training

New hires must complete the Mimecast Awareness Training within 10 business days of their start date.

### 4.2 Annual Training

All active personnel must complete annual security awareness refresher training by November 30 of each year. The training window opens August 1.

### 4.3 Completion Target

The organization targets 100% completion by the November 30 deadline. Any employee who has not completed training by December 15 will have their system access suspended.

## 5. Acceptable Use Policy

### 5.1 AUP Acknowledgment

All personnel must sign the Acceptable Use Policy before receiving system credentials. Signed copies are stored in Workday.

## 6. Role Changes

### 6.1 Access Review

Role changes trigger an automated access review via Workday integration with Okta. The IT Security team completes the review within 5 business days.

## 7. Termination

All access is revoked within 24 hours of termination.
