## Key Exclusions

- Acts of war, terrorism, or nation-state cyber operations
- Known pre-existing vulnerabilities left unpatched beyond 60 days after patch availability
- Intentional or fraudulent acts by insured's officers or directors
- Infrastructure failure not caused by a cyber event
- Contractual liability beyond statutory obligations
- Cryptocurrency and digital asset losses
