## 7. Vendor Security Incident Response

### 7.1 Notification Requirements

Vendors must notify Solaris Tech of security incidents within 72 hours of detection per contractual obligations.

### 7.2 Internal Escalation

Upon receiving vendor incident notification:
1. Security Operations acknowledges receipt within 1 hour
2. Incident classified using standard severity matrix
3. If customer data potentially impacted: escalate to CISO and Legal within 4 hours
4. CISO determines customer notification requirements within 24 hours

### 7.3 Vendor Incident Tracking

All vendor incidents are logged in the incident management system with:
- Vendor name and contact
- Incident description and timeline
- Impact assessment
- Remediation actions taken by vendor
- Follow-up requirements

### 7.4 Post-Incident Review

A post-incident review is conducted within 30 days for any vendor incident affecting customer data. The review assesses:
- Root cause adequacy
- Vendor remediation effectiveness
- Need for additional controls or vendor reassessment
