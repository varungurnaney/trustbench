# Human Resources Security Policy

**Document ID:** POL-HR-012
**Version:** 2.8
**Effective Date:** March 1, 2025
**Owner:** Human Resources Department
**Classification:** Internal
**Last Reviewed:** February 20, 2025

---

## 1. Purpose

This policy establishes the personnel security requirements for Vanguard Data Systems to ensure all individuals accessing company information systems are properly vetted, trained, and managed throughout their employment lifecycle.

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to all employees, contractors, and temporary staff of Vanguard Data Systems.

## 3. Pre-Employment Screening

### 3.1 Background Checks

All candidates must pass a background check conducted by HireRight before their start date. Results must be adjudicated by HR before system access is provisioned.

### 3.2 Onboarding Checklist

Each new hire must complete all items on the Employee Onboarding Security Checklist (FORM-HR-009) within their first 5 business days, including security awareness training, AUP signing, and equipment acknowledgment.

## 4. Security Awareness Training

### 4.1 Initial Training

All new employees and contractors must complete the Proofpoint Security Awareness Training module within 5 business days of their start date. Completion is tracked in the Proofpoint platform.

### 4.2 Annual Training

All active employees must complete annual security awareness refresher training. The training window opens September 1 and closes November 30 each year.

### 4.3 Completion Requirement

100% completion is required. The CISO reviews the completion report by December 15 and escalates any non-completion to the department head.

## 5. Role Changes and Internal Transfers

### 5.1 Access Review Trigger

When an employee transfers to a new role, changes departments, or receives a promotion, an access review must be triggered automatically via the HR Information System (BambooHR). The HRIS role-change event generates a ServiceNow ticket (category: Access Review — Role Change) assigned to the IT Security team.

### 5.2 Review Timeline

The IT Security team must complete the access review within 5 business days of the role change effective date. The review must document:

- Previous role and access entitlements
- New role and required access entitlements
- Access to be revoked
- Access to be granted
- Reviewer name and sign-off date

### 5.3 Excessive Access Prevention

Any access privileges not required by the new role must be revoked within 2 business days of the review completion.

## 6. Termination

### 6.1 Access Revocation

All access must be revoked within 24 hours of the termination effective date.

## 7. Roles and Responsibilities

| Role | Responsibility |
|------|---------------|
| CHRO | Policy owner, escalation authority |
| HR Operations | Onboarding, HRIS maintenance, role change processing |
| IT Security | Access reviews, training compliance monitoring |
| Hiring Managers | Onboarding checklist completion |
| CISO | Training completion review, escalation |
