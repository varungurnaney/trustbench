# Physical Security Policy

**Document ID:** POL-PS-010
**Version:** 3.0
**Effective Date:** January 10, 2025
**Owner:** Facilities and Security Operations
**Classification:** Internal

---

## 1. Purpose

This policy defines Nimbus Cloud Solutions' physical security requirements to protect personnel, facilities, and information assets from unauthorized physical access, theft, and environmental hazards.

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to Nimbus Cloud Solutions' corporate headquarters at 5500 Lakewood Blvd, Suite 300, Denver, CO, including all office floors, server rooms, and common areas within the leased space.

## 3. Facility Security Zones

### 3.1 Zone Definitions

| Zone | Classification | Areas |
|------|---------------|-------|
| Public | Unrestricted | Building lobby (managed by landlord), parking garage |
| General Office | Controlled | Suite 300 main entrance, open office areas, conference rooms, break room |
| Restricted | High Control | Server Room A, Server Room B, network distribution closet, records storage |
| Executive | High Control | C-suite offices, board room |

### 3.2 Access Requirements

- **Public:** No Nimbus badge required (building access managed by property management)
- **General Office:** Valid Nimbus employee badge
- **Restricted:** Employee badge with explicit Restricted zone authorization (approved by Infrastructure Director)
- **Executive:** Employee badge with Executive zone authorization (approved by CEO)

## 4. Badge Access Controls

### 4.1 Badge Issuance

New employee badges are provisioned by the Facilities Coordinator within 1 business day of the employee's start date. Access levels are assigned per the Access Authorization Form signed by the hiring manager.

### 4.2 Badge Types

| Type | Identifier | Scope |
|------|-----------|-------|
| Full-Time Employee | Blue badge, "FTE" prefix | Per authorization form |
| Contractor | Yellow badge, "CTR" prefix | Sponsor-approved areas only |
| Visitor | Orange badge, "VIS" prefix | General Office only, escorted |

### 4.3 Access Modifications

Changes to badge access levels require a new Access Authorization Form approved by the employee's current manager and, for Restricted zones, the Infrastructure Director.

### 4.4 Quarterly Access Review

The Security Manager conducts quarterly reviews of all badge access authorizations. Access no longer required for job function is revoked within 5 business days of the review.

### 4.5 Badge Deactivation on Termination

Human Resources must notify the Facilities Coordinator of all employee terminations (voluntary or involuntary) at least 4 business hours before or immediately upon the termination effective time. The Facilities Coordinator deactivates the badge within 4 business hours of receiving HR notification. For involuntary terminations, badge deactivation occurs before the termination meeting concludes. The terminated employee's manager is responsible for collecting the physical badge.

## 5. Surveillance

### 5.1 CCTV System

Nimbus Cloud Solutions operates a Verkada cloud-managed camera system with cameras at the suite entrance, server room doors, and the network closet hallway.

### 5.2 Retention

CCTV footage is retained for 90 days in the Verkada cloud platform. Footage related to active security incidents is preserved until the investigation is formally closed.

### 5.3 Access to Footage

CCTV footage may only be reviewed by the Security Manager, CISO, or Legal upon documented request with stated justification.

## 6. Visitor and Vendor Management

### 6.1 Pre-Registration

All visitors must be pre-registered by the host employee using the visitor management system at least 24 hours in advance. Emergency visits require approval from the Security Manager.

### 6.2 Check-In Process

Visitors present government-issued photo identification at reception. Identity is verified against the pre-registration record. A temporary visitor badge is issued.

### 6.3 Escort Requirement

All non-employees, including vendors and contractors, must be escorted by the designated host employee at all times while on premises. Under no circumstances shall a visitor or vendor be granted unescorted access to Restricted zones. The host employee is personally responsible for the visitor's actions and must remain within line of sight.

### 6.4 Vendor-Specific Requirements

Vendors performing maintenance or service work in Restricted zones must have:
- A current vendor services agreement on file
- Pre-approved scope of work for the visit
- Host employee escort throughout the visit
- All tools and equipment inspected upon entry and exit

### 6.5 Check-Out

Visitor badges are returned at reception. Departure time is logged. Badges not returned by end of business are deactivated and reported to the Security Manager.

## 7. After-Hours Access

### 7.1 Standard Business Hours

Standard business hours are Monday through Friday, 7:00 AM to 7:00 PM MST.

### 7.2 After-Hours Authorization

Employee access outside standard business hours requires pre-approval from the employee's manager via the After-Hours Access Request Form. Approved after-hours access is logged in the access control system and reviewed weekly by the Security Manager.

### 7.3 Exceptions

On-call personnel with documented on-call rotation schedules are pre-authorized for after-hours access during their on-call shifts. On-call schedules must be submitted to Facilities monthly.
