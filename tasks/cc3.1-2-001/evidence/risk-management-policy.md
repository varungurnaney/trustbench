## 1. Purpose

This policy establishes the enterprise risk management framework for CloudVault Inc. to identify, assess, manage, and monitor risks in compliance with SOC 2 Trust Services Criteria CC3.1.

## 2. Scope

All business units, information systems, and third-party relationships.

## 3. Risk Register

### 3.1 Maintenance

All identified risks must be recorded in the risk register (ServiceNow GRC module). Each entry must include:
- Unique risk identifier
- Risk description and category
- Likelihood and impact ratings (5x5 matrix)
- Risk classification (Low / Medium / High / Critical)
- Treatment strategy and plan
- Risk owner (named individual)
- Last review date and next review date

### 3.2 Review Cadence

Risk owners must review their assigned risks according to the following schedule:

| Risk Classification | Review Frequency | Maximum Review Interval |
|---------------------|------------------|------------------------|
| Critical | Quarterly | 90 days |
| High | Quarterly | 90 days |
| Medium | Semi-annually | 180 days |
| Low | Annually | 365 days |

Overdue reviews must be escalated to the CRO within 5 business days.

## 4. Risk Rating Methodology

### 4.1 5x5 Matrix

Risk Rating = Likelihood x Impact

| Rating Range | Classification |
|-------------|----------------|
| 1-4 | Low |
| 5-9 | Medium |
| 10-15 | High |
| 16-25 | Critical |

## 5. Risk Treatment

| Strategy | Approval Required |
|----------|-------------------|
| Mitigate | Risk Owner |
| Transfer | CRO + CFO |
| Accept | CISO for High; Board for Critical |
| Avoid | Business Unit VP + CRO |

## 6. Governance

The Security Governance Committee meets quarterly to review the risk register, approve treatment plans, and assess emerging risks.
