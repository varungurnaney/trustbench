# Luminos EdTech — Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2025
**Version:** 3.0

---

## 1. Introduction

Luminos EdTech Ltd ("Luminos," "we," "us") operates an online learning management platform used by universities, professional development organizations, and corporate training departments. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect personal information when you use the Luminos Learning Platform (learn.luminos.io), visit our website (www.luminos.io), or interact with our services.

Luminos is headquartered in London, United Kingdom and processes data under the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the EU General Data Protection Regulation (EU GDPR).

## 2. Information We Collect

**Information You Provide:**
- Account registration: name, email address, institution or employer, role (student/instructor/administrator)
- Profile information: profile photo, bio, timezone, language preference
- Learning content: assignments submitted, discussion forum posts, quiz responses
- Communication: support tickets, feedback, survey responses

**Information Collected Automatically:**
- Technical data: IP address, browser type, device type, operating system
- Learning activity data: courses enrolled, modules accessed, session duration, completion status
- Platform usage: features used, navigation paths, search queries

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We process your personal information for the following purposes:

- **Service Delivery:** Providing the learning platform, course delivery, and certification
- **Learning Analytics:** We use analytics tools under our legitimate interest in improving our educational platform. You can opt out of analytics tracking in your account settings.
- **Assessment:** Grading, progress tracking, and certification issuance
- **Communication:** Course notifications, assignment reminders, platform updates
- **Support:** Responding to technical and administrative inquiries
- **Compliance:** Meeting legal, regulatory, and accreditation requirements

## 4. Legal Basis for Processing

- **Contractual Necessity:** Core platform services, course delivery, certification
- **Legitimate Interest:** Platform improvement, analytics, security, fraud detection
- **Consent:** Marketing communications, optional survey participation
- **Legal Obligation:** Accreditation records, regulatory reporting

## 5. Data Sharing

We share personal information with the following categories of service providers:

- **Cloud Infrastructure:** AWS (hosting in eu-west-2 London, eu-central-1 Frankfurt)
- **Analytics:** Mixpanel for platform usage analytics (anonymized where possible)
- **Communication:** SendGrid for email notifications
- **Support:** Intercom for in-app support chat
- **Payment Processing:** Stripe for subscription billing

All providers are bound by Data Processing Agreements.

When Luminos is used by universities or corporate training departments, the institution is the data controller and Luminos acts as a data processor. In these cases, the institution's privacy policy governs the use of learner data and Luminos processes data only as instructed.

## 6. Data Retention

We retain your learning data for 2 years after your last platform activity, after which it is permanently deleted. Account data is retained for the duration of your active account plus 12 months. Certification records may be retained longer to support verification requests.

## 7. Your Rights

Under applicable data protection laws, you have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Rectify inaccurate data
- Request erasure of your data
- Restrict processing
- Data portability
- Object to processing
- Withdraw consent

To exercise these rights, contact privacy@luminos.io.

## 9. Children's Privacy

Luminos is designed for users aged 16 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If we discover that a child under 16 has provided personal information, we will delete it promptly.

Material changes will be communicated via email at least 30 days before taking effect.
