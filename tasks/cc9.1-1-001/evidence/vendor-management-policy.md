# Third-Party Vendor Management Policy

**Document ID:** POL-VRM-001
**Version:** 1.4
**Effective Date:** April 1, 2025
**Owner:** IT Operations
**Classification:** Internal

---

## 1. Purpose

This policy establishes the requirements for managing relationships with third-party vendors that provide services to Meridian Cloud Inc.

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to all vendors engaged by Meridian Cloud, including cloud service providers, SaaS applications, professional services firms, and contractors.

## 3. Vendor Onboarding

Prior to engaging a new vendor:
- A security questionnaire must be completed by the vendor
- Legal review of the vendor contract must be performed
- The vendor must be registered in the vendor tracking spreadsheet

## 4. Ongoing Vendor Monitoring

Vendors shall be assessed periodically based on business need. Assessment activities include:
- Reviewing the vendor's most recent SOC 2 Type II report (if available)
- Sending a security questionnaire for vendors without SOC 2 reports
- Confirming that vendor contacts are up to date

## 5. Contractual Requirements

All vendor contracts should include the following provisions where applicable:
- Data protection and confidentiality obligations
- Security incident notification within a reasonable timeframe
- Data return or destruction upon contract termination
- Cyber insurance coverage for vendors handling sensitive data

## 6. Vendor Offboarding

When a vendor relationship is terminated:
- All access credentials and accounts are disabled
- Data return or destruction is confirmed via email
- The vendor tracking spreadsheet is updated

## 7. Roles and Responsibilities

| Role | Responsibility |
|------|---------------|
| IT Operations Lead | Vendor onboarding, tracking spreadsheet maintenance |
| Legal | Contract review |
| CISO | Policy approval |

## 8. Policy Review

This policy is reviewed annually.
