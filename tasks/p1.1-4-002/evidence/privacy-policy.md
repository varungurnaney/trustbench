# Nextera Health — Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** July 1, 2025
**Version:** 4.0
**Applicable Jurisdiction:** European Union, United Kingdom, United States (HIPAA-adjacent)

---

## 1. Introduction

Nextera Health Ltd ("Nextera Health," "we," "us") operates a digital health and wellness platform that provides personalized health assessments, wellness tracking, and lifestyle recommendations. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information, including sensitive health information, when you use our platform (app.nexterahealth.com), mobile applications, or visit our website.

Given the sensitive nature of health information, we apply enhanced protections to all health-related personal data processed through our platform.

## 2. Information We Collect

**Information You Provide:**
- Account registration: name, email, date of birth, gender, country of residence
- Health profile: self-reported health conditions, medications, allergies, lifestyle habits
- Health assessments: responses to health questionnaires, risk assessment results, wellness scores
- Communication: support inquiries, feedback, survey responses

**Information Collected Automatically:**
- Technical data: IP address, device type, operating system, browser version
- Platform usage: features accessed, assessment modules completed, session duration
- Location data: country-level geolocation derived from IP address (not precise location)

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We process your personal information for the following purposes:

- Delivering personalized health assessments and wellness recommendations
- Managing your account and subscription
- Improving our health assessment algorithms and platform features
- Sending service-related communications (assessment results, reminders, account alerts)
- Conducting anonymized health research (with separate consent)
- Ensuring platform security and preventing unauthorized access
- Complying with legal and regulatory requirements

## 4. Legal Basis for Processing

- **Consent:** Health assessment data, marketing communications, research participation
- **Contractual Necessity:** Account management, service delivery, subscription billing
- **Legitimate Interest:** Platform improvement, security, fraud prevention
- **Legal Obligation:** Regulatory compliance, audit requirements

## 5. Your Choices

You have the following choices regarding your data:

- **Marketing Communications:** You can opt out of marketing emails at any time using the unsubscribe link in each email.
- **Analytics Tracking:** You can opt out of non-essential analytics tracking at any time through our cookie preferences panel. We respect your choice and will not track your usage for analytics purposes once you opt out.
- **Health Research:** Participation in anonymized health research is voluntary and requires separate explicit consent.
- **Account Deletion:** You can request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting privacy@nexterahealth.com.

## 6. Data Retention

We retain your personal health information for a maximum of 3 years after your last interaction with the platform, after which it is securely deleted. Account data is retained for the duration of your active subscription plus 12 months. Payment records are retained for 7 years to comply with tax and accounting regulations.

## 7. Data Sharing

We share personal information with the following categories of service providers, all bound by Data Processing Agreements:

- **Cloud Infrastructure:** AWS for hosting and data storage
- **Payment Processing:** Stripe for subscription billing
- **Email Delivery:** SendGrid for transactional emails
- **Customer Support:** Intercom for in-app support chat

We do not sell your personal information or share it with advertisers.

## 8. Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures appropriate for health data:

- AES-256 encryption at rest for all health-related data
- TLS 1.3 for all data in transit
- Role-based access controls with principle of least privilege
- Annual penetration testing and quarterly vulnerability assessments
- SOC 2 Type II audit in progress

We will notify you of material changes via email at least 30 days before they take effect.
