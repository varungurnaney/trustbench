# Employee Satisfaction Survey — 2025 Annual Results Summary

**Report Period:** October 2025 Survey
**Prepared by:** Human Resources
**Distribution:** Executive Leadership Team
**Classification:** Internal
**Organization:** Crestwood Dynamics

---

## Overview

The annual employee satisfaction survey was conducted October 1-15, 2025 with a 78% response rate (312 of 400 employees).

## Overall Results

| Category | 2024 Score | 2025 Score | Change | Benchmark |
|----------|-----------|-----------|--------|-----------|
| Overall Satisfaction | 4.1 / 5.0 | 3.9 / 5.0 | -0.2 | 4.0 |
| Work-Life Balance | 3.8 / 5.0 | 3.6 / 5.0 | -0.2 | 3.7 |
| Career Development | 3.5 / 5.0 | 3.7 / 5.0 | +0.2 | 3.6 |
| Management Effectiveness | 4.0 / 5.0 | 3.8 / 5.0 | -0.2 | 3.9 |
| Compensation & Benefits | 3.6 / 5.0 | 3.5 / 5.0 | -0.1 | 3.5 |
| Company Culture | 4.2 / 5.0 | 4.0 / 5.0 | -0.2 | 4.0 |
| Communication | 3.9 / 5.0 | 3.7 / 5.0 | -0.2 | 3.8 |

## Department Highlights

### Engineering (N=95)
- Highest satisfaction with career development (4.1)
- Concerns about on-call rotation burden
- Request for more hackathon days

### Sales (N=52)
- Highest overall satisfaction (4.3)
- Positive response to new CRM implementation
- Request for better cross-team communication

### Customer Success (N=38)
- Lowest satisfaction with work-life balance (3.2)
- Concerns about ticket volume and staffing levels
- Positive response to new knowledge base system

### IT Operations (N=28)
- Moderate satisfaction across all categories
- Concerns about technical debt in legacy systems
- Positive response to cloud migration initiative

## Key Themes from Open-Ended Responses

1. **Remote Work Flexibility** (mentioned by 45% of respondents): Strong preference for maintaining hybrid work policy
2. **Cross-Department Communication** (mentioned by 32%): Desire for better visibility into other teams' priorities
3. **Professional Development Budget** (mentioned by 28%): Request to increase training budget from $1,500 to $2,500 per employee
4. **Office Amenities** (mentioned by 22%): Requests for better snack options and standing desks

## Turnover Correlation

Departments with satisfaction scores below 3.5 in any category showed 15% higher voluntary turnover in the 6 months following the 2024 survey.
