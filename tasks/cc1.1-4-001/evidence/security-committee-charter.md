## 1. Purpose

The Information Security Governance Committee (ISGC) provides strategic oversight and direction for Crestwood Dynamics' information security program. The committee ensures that security practices align with business objectives, regulatory requirements, and the organization's commitment to integrity and ethical values.

## 2. Authority

The ISGC is authorized by the Board of Directors to:

- Approve and enforce information security policies
- Review and accept enterprise security risks
- Direct security program investments and priorities
- Oversee regulatory compliance and audit activities
- Ensure the organization maintains a commitment to ethical business practices and data stewardship

### 3.2 Quorum Requirements

A quorum of three (3) of five (5) voting members must be present for the committee to conduct official business. All policy approvals and risk acceptance decisions require quorum.

### 3.3 Alternate Procedures

In extraordinary circumstances where a quarterly meeting cannot be held, the CISO may conduct an asynchronous review via email, provided:

- Written approval is obtained from quorum (3 of 5 members)
- All agenda materials are distributed at least 5 business days in advance
- Responses are documented and retained as meeting records
- The asynchronous review is ratified at the next in-person meeting

## 4. Meeting Cadence

### 4.1 Regular Meetings

The ISGC meets quarterly. Regular meetings are scheduled for the third Wednesday of January, April, July, and October.

### 4.2 Special Meetings

The Chair or CISO may convene special meetings with 72 hours notice for urgent security matters.

## 5. Standing Agenda

Each quarterly meeting includes:

1. Approval of prior meeting minutes
2. Security metrics dashboard review (all KPIs)
3. Enterprise risk register review
4. Policy review and approval pipeline
5. Incident and compliance update
6. Open items and new business

## 6. Reporting

### 6.1 Board Reporting

The Committee Chair provides a quarterly summary to the full Board of Directors.

### 6.2 Documentation

Meeting minutes are prepared by the GRC team and distributed within 5 business days.
