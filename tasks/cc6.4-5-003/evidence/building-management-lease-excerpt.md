# Building Lease Agreement — Relevant Excerpts

**Tenant:** Atlas Cloud Services, Inc.
**Landlord:** Technology Plaza Holdings, LLC
**Property:** 1800 Technology Plaza, Suite 420, Seattle, WA 98101
**Managed by:** Cascade Property Group
**Lease Term:** May 1, 2023 – April 30, 2028
**Document:** Excerpt from Lease Agreement, Sections 12-14 (Security and Access)

---

## Section 12: Building Access and Security

### 12.1 Business Hours Access

The Building shall be open to Tenant, its employees, and authorized visitors during Standard Business Hours (6:00 AM to 9:00 PM, Monday through Friday, and 8:00 AM to 1:00 PM, Saturday). During Standard Business Hours, the Building lobby shall be accessible without access card authentication.

### 12.2 After-Hours Access

After Standard Business Hours, Building access requires a Cascade Property Group access card issued to Tenant's authorized employees. Tenant shall provide Landlord with a current list of authorized after-hours access cardholders, updated within 30 days of personnel changes.

### 12.3 Building Security Personnel

Landlord shall provide a uniformed security guard at the Building lobby reception desk during Standard Business Hours. After Standard Business Hours, Building security is provided by a roving patrol service conducting rounds every 2 hours.

### 12.4 Stairwell Access

Per City of Seattle Fire Code (Seattle Fire Code Chapter 10, Section 1010.1.4), all stairwell doors shall remain unlocked from both the stairwell side and the hallway side to facilitate emergency egress and re-entry. Landlord may not restrict stairwell access via badge readers or locks without Fire Marshal approval. As of the date of this lease, no Fire Marshal variance has been granted for stairwell access restrictions in this Building.

### 12.5 Tenant Security Improvements

Tenant may install, at Tenant's sole expense, additional security measures within Tenant's Premises (Suite 420) including but not limited to: access control readers, CCTV cameras, intrusion detection, and alarm systems. All installations must comply with Building standards and be approved by Landlord in writing prior to installation. Tenant-installed security equipment shall remain Tenant's property.

## Section 13: Common Areas

### 13.1 Shared Spaces

Common areas of the Building, including the lobby, elevator lobbies, hallways, restrooms on non-tenant floors, stairwells, parking garage, loading dock, and rooftop terrace, are shared among all tenants. Landlord manages access to common areas and is responsible for common area maintenance, cleaning, and security.

### 13.2 Elevator Access

Landlord shall install badge readers at each floor's elevator lobby to restrict elevator access to authorized tenants of that floor. The 4th floor elevator lobby reader (AP-ELEV-04) restricts elevator egress to cardholders authorized for the 4th floor. Badge readers do not restrict elevator boarding from the lobby level.

## Section 14: Parking

### 14.1 Reserved Parking

Tenant is allocated 15 reserved parking spaces on Level P2 of the parking garage. Parking garage is accessible during Standard Business Hours without restriction and after hours via building access card.
