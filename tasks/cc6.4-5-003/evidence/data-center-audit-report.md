## 1. Purpose

Annual physical security audit of NorthStar Data Centers as required by Atlas Cloud Services Physical Security Policy POL-PS-030, Section 8.2. This audit independently verifies that the colocation provider's physical security controls meet Atlas's requirements and are operating effectively.

## 2. Scope

Physical inspection of NorthStar's Tukwila facility with focus on:
- Perimeter and building access controls
- Atlas cabinet security (CAB-N3-422, CAB-N3-423)
- CCTV coverage of Atlas cabinet row
- Environmental controls
- Authorized access list verification
