# Information Security Policy

**Document ID:** POL-ISEC-002
**Version:** 3.1
**Effective Date:** April 15, 2024
**Owner:** Chief Information Security Officer
**Classification:** Internal
**Organization:** Atherton SaaS Inc.

---

## 1. Purpose

This policy establishes the information security requirements and governance framework for Atherton SaaS Inc. to protect customer data, business information, and technology assets.

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to all employees, contractors, temporary workers, and third-party service providers who access Atherton SaaS Inc. information systems.

## 3. Governance Structure

### 3.1 Security Steering Committee

The Security Steering Committee provides executive oversight of the information security program. The committee meets quarterly to review security posture, approve policies, and direct security investments.

### 3.2 CISO Authority

The CISO has authority to:

- Approve and enforce security policies and standards
- Direct incident response activities
- Approve security exceptions (up to 90 days)
- Report security posture to the Board of Directors

## 4. Policy Framework

### 4.1 Policy Hierarchy

- Tier 1: Information Security Policy (this document)
- Tier 2: Security Standards (encryption, access control, network security)
- Tier 3: Procedures and Guidelines
- Tier 4: Operational Runbooks

### 4.2 Policy Review Cycle

All Tier 1 and Tier 2 policies are reviewed annually by the CISO and approved by the Security Steering Committee. Policy reviews are tracked in the Policy Review Register.

### 4.3 Policy Distribution

Approved policies are published on the company intranet. All employees are notified of policy changes via email.

## 5. Security Controls

### 5.1 Access Management

- Principle of least privilege for all system access
- Multi-factor authentication for all production and cloud console access
- Quarterly access reviews for critical systems
- Immediate access revocation upon termination

### 5.2 Data Protection

- Data classification: Public, Internal, Confidential, Restricted
- Encryption at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.2+)
- Data loss prevention controls on email and cloud storage
- Annual data retention review

### 5.3 Vulnerability Management

- Weekly automated vulnerability scans
- Critical vulnerabilities remediated within 72 hours
- High vulnerabilities remediated within 30 days
- Quarterly penetration testing by approved third party

### 5.4 Incident Response

- 24/7 SOC monitoring via SIEM platform
- Incident classification (P1-P4) with defined response times
- P1/P2 incidents reported to CISO within 1 hour
- Post-incident reviews completed within 5 business days

## 6. Security Metrics

The CISO reports the following security metrics to the Security Steering Committee quarterly:

1. Mean time to detect (MTTD) security incidents
2. Mean time to respond (MTTR) to security incidents
3. Vulnerability remediation compliance rate
4. Security awareness training completion rate
5. Number of policy exceptions and their status

## 7. Compliance

This policy supports compliance with SOC 2 Trust Services Criteria, GDPR, and CCPA requirements.

## 8. Enforcement

Violations are subject to disciplinary action. Security incidents caused by policy non-compliance are tracked and reported to HR.
