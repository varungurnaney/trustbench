# Physical Security Policy

**Document ID:** POL-PS-012
**Version:** 5.1
**Effective Date:** April 1, 2025
**Owner:** Security Operations
**Classification:** Internal

---

## 1. Purpose

This policy establishes Crestline Analytics' physical security controls to protect personnel, facilities, equipment, and information assets from unauthorized physical access, damage, theft, and interference.

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to all Crestline Analytics facilities including the corporate headquarters and primary operations center at 7800 Westpark Drive, Suite 200, McLean, VA.

## 3. Facility Security Zones

### 3.1 Zone Definitions

| Zone | Classification | Areas |
|------|---------------|-------|
| Public | Open | Building lobby, visitor parking |
| General Office | Badge Required | Suite 200 main entrance, open floor, conference rooms, break room |
| Restricted | Badge + Authorization | Server Room A, Server Room B, Network Closet, records vault |
| Executive | Badge + Authorization | C-suite offices, secure conference room |

### 3.2 Access Requirements

- **Public:** No Crestline badge required
- **General Office:** Valid Crestline employee or contractor badge
- **Restricted:** Badge with explicit Restricted zone authorization approved by Infrastructure Director
- **Executive:** Badge with Executive zone authorization approved by COO

## 4. Badge Management

### 4.1 Employee Badges

Employee badges are issued by the Facilities Coordinator upon receipt of a completed Access Authorization Form (AAF) approved by the employee's manager. Restricted zone access requires additional approval from the Infrastructure Director.

### 4.2 Badge Types

| Badge Type | Prefix | Access |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| Full-Time Employee | B- | Per AAF |
| Contractor | CTR- | Per contract scope and approved work order |
| Visitor | VIS- | General Office only, escorted |

### 4.3 Termination Procedures

Upon employee separation, HR notifies the Facilities Coordinator. Badge deactivation must occur within 2 business hours of the termination effective time. The badge is physically collected during the exit process.

### 4.4 Contractor Access Controls

Contractor badges are provisioned for the duration of the contract engagement and limited to the areas and hours defined in the contractor's approved work order. Contractor access is reviewed monthly. All contractor access to Restricted zones requires escort by the sponsoring Crestline employee unless the work order explicitly authorizes unescorted access during standard business hours only.

## 5. Surveillance

### 5.1 CCTV System

Crestline Analytics uses Axis IP cameras managed through Milestone XProtect VMS. Cameras cover all entry points, server rooms, hallways, and the parking garage. Cameras record 24/7 at 1080p.

### 5.2 Retention

CCTV footage is retained for 90 days. Incident-related footage is preserved until investigation closure.

### 5.3 Monitoring

Live CCTV monitoring during business hours by reception. After-hours monitoring via motion-detection alerts to on-call Security.

## 6. Visitor Management

### 6.1 Pre-Registration and Escort

All visitors must be pre-registered and escorted by the host employee at all times. Visitors are restricted to the General Office zone unless explicitly authorized by the Infrastructure Director for Restricted zone escorted access.

## 7. After-Hours Access

### 7.1 Standard Business Hours

Monday through Friday, 7:00 AM to 7:00 PM Eastern Time.

### 7.2 After-Hours Authorization

Access to Crestline facilities outside standard business hours requires pre-approval from the employee's manager using the After-Hours Access Request Form. Approved requests are logged in the after-hours exception register. The Security Manager reviews all after-hours access weekly.

### 7.3 On-Call Exceptions

On-call personnel with documented on-call rotation schedules approved by the Infrastructure Director are pre-authorized for after-hours access to General Office and Restricted zones during their on-call shifts. On-call schedules must be submitted to the Facilities Coordinator by the 25th of the preceding month.

### 7.4 Contractor After-Hours

Contractors may not access facilities outside standard business hours unless the specific after-hours work is documented in an approved work order and a separate After-Hours Access Request Form is submitted by the contractor's Crestline sponsor.

## 8. Environmental Controls

### 8.1 Fire Suppression

All server rooms are equipped with FM-200 clean agent fire suppression. Systems are tested annually per NFPA 2001 standards.

### 8.2 Environmental Monitoring

Temperature and humidity sensors in server rooms report to the Schneider Electric BMS. Alert thresholds: temperature above 77°F, humidity above 55%.
