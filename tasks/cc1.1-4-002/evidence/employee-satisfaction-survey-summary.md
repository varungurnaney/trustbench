# Employee Satisfaction Survey — 2025 Mid-Year Pulse

**Report Period:** June 2025 Pulse Survey
**Prepared by:** Human Resources
**Distribution:** Executive Leadership Team
**Classification:** Internal
**Organization:** Crestwood Dynamics

---

## Overview

A mid-year pulse survey was conducted June 1-10, 2025 with a 72% response rate (288 of 400 employees). This is a shortened version of the annual survey focusing on key engagement indicators.

## Results Summary

| Category | Score | Benchmark | Status |
|----------|-------|-----------|--------|
| Overall Engagement | 4.0 / 5.0 | 3.9 | Above Benchmark |
| Manager Relationship | 4.1 / 5.0 | 4.0 | Above Benchmark |
| Team Collaboration | 4.2 / 5.0 | 3.8 | Above Benchmark |
| Workload Balance | 3.4 / 5.0 | 3.6 | Below Benchmark |
| Growth Opportunities | 3.8 / 5.0 | 3.7 | Above Benchmark |
| Company Direction | 3.9 / 5.0 | 3.8 | Above Benchmark |

## Top Themes

1. **Merger Uncertainty** (mentioned by 38%): Employees seeking clarity on integration timeline and impact
2. **Workload Concerns** (mentioned by 31%): Several teams report increased workload from SOC 2 preparation
3. **Positive Culture** (mentioned by 44%): Strong appreciation for team culture and collaboration

## Department Insights

- **Security Team (N=14):** Engagement 4.3, highest workload concern (SOC 2 audit prep). 3 of 14 considering external opportunities.
- **Engineering (N=95):** Engagement 4.1, positive about new tooling investments
- **Sales (N=52):** Engagement 3.8, concerns about merger impact on territories

## Recommendations

1. Executive communication session on merger timeline (scheduled for July all-hands)
2. Review workload distribution for security and compliance teams
3. Conduct stay interviews for at-risk high performers
