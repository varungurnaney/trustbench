## 1. Purpose

Annual physical security audit of SecureVault Colocation as required by Meridian Software Physical Security Policy POL-PS-022, Section 8.2. This audit verifies that the colocation provider's physical security controls meet Meridian's requirements and are operating effectively.

## 2. Scope

Physical inspection of SecureVault's Hillsboro facility with focus on:
- Perimeter and building access controls
- Meridian cabinet security (CAB-H3-205, CAB-H3-206)
- CCTV coverage and retention
- Environmental controls
- Access list accuracy
