# Vendor Security Questionnaire Summary — Q4 2025

**Prepared by:** Procurement Team
**Date:** December 15, 2025

---

## Overview

This document summarizes the security questionnaire responses received from Orion Cloud Services' key vendors during Q4 2025. All vendors with access to production systems or customer data are required to complete an annual security questionnaire.

## Vendor Responses

### AWS (Amazon Web Services)
- **Questionnaire Status:** Completed (SOC 2 Type II report provided in lieu)
- **SOC 2 Report Date:** September 2025
- **Key Controls:** Encryption at rest (AES-256), encryption in transit (TLS 1.2+), MFA for console access, 24/7 security monitoring
- **Risk Rating:** Low
- **Next Review:** Q4 2026

### Stripe Inc.
- **Questionnaire Status:** Completed (PCI DSS Level 1 certification provided)
- **Certification Date:** March 2025
- **Key Controls:** PCI DSS compliant, tokenization of card data, SOC 1 and SOC 2 reports available
- **Risk Rating:** Low
- **Next Review:** Q4 2026

### Cloudflare Inc.
- **Questionnaire Status:** Completed
- **SOC 2 Report Date:** June 2025
- **Key Controls:** DDoS mitigation, WAF rules, SSL/TLS termination, ISO 27001 certified
- **Risk Rating:** Low
- **Next Review:** Q4 2026

### Datadog Inc.
- **Questionnaire Status:** Completed
- **SOC 2 Report Date:** August 2025
- **Key Controls:** Role-based access control, data encryption, audit logging, SOC 2 Type II
- **Risk Rating:** Low
- **Next Review:** Q4 2026

## Summary

All 4 vendors completed their annual security questionnaire or provided equivalent certification. No high-risk findings were identified. All vendors maintain current SOC 2 or equivalent certifications.
