# Orion Cloud Services — Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** June 1, 2025
**Version:** 5.0

---

## 1. Introduction

Orion Cloud Services AB ("Orion," "we," "us") is a cloud-based enterprise collaboration platform headquartered in Stockholm, Sweden. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and protect personal information when you use the Orion Platform, visit our website (www.orioncloud.eu), or interact with our services.

We are committed to transparency and compliance with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other applicable data protection laws.

## 2. Data Controller

Orion Cloud Services AB acts as the data controller for personal information processed in connection with our website, account management, and direct customer relationships. When processing data on behalf of our enterprise customers, Orion acts as a data processor under the terms of our Data Processing Agreement.

## 3. Information We Collect

We collect and process the following categories of personal information:

- **Account Data:** Name, email address, company name, job title, phone number, billing address
- **Usage Data:** Login timestamps, features accessed, session duration, IP addresses, browser and device information
- **Communication Data:** Support tickets, emails, chat transcripts, feedback submissions
- **Analytics Data:** Feature usage patterns, click paths, session recordings (with consent), error reports
- **Payment Data:** Billing information managed by Stripe; we do not store payment card numbers

## 4. How We Use Your Information

We use personal information to:
- Provide, maintain, and improve the Orion Platform
- Process transactions and manage subscriptions
- Provide customer support and respond to inquiries
- Analyze usage patterns to improve our services
- Ensure platform security and detect fraud
- Comply with legal obligations

## 5. Data Sharing

We share personal information with the following categories of service providers:

- **Infrastructure Providers:** AWS (hosting), Cloudflare (CDN and DDoS protection)
- **Payment Processors:** Stripe for subscription billing
- **Analytics Providers:** Internal analytics pipeline; anonymized data only
- **Communication Tools:** For delivering service notifications and support

All service providers are bound by Data Processing Agreements and process data only on our instructions.

## 6. International Data Transfers

Our primary infrastructure is located in the European Economic Area (AWS eu-north-1, Stockholm). Where data transfers outside the EEA are necessary, we rely on Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) as approved by the European Commission, or adequacy decisions where applicable.

## 7. Exercising Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict processing of, and port your personal data. You also have the right to object to processing and to withdraw consent.

To exercise any of these rights, submit a request through our privacy portal at privacy.orioncloud.eu/request or email dsar@orioncloud.eu.

We will respond to all data subject access requests within 30 calendar days of receipt. If we cannot verify your identity, we will contact you within 5 business days to request additional verification.

## 8. Data Retention

We retain personal information for the duration of your account plus 24 months after closure. Specific retention periods by data category are documented in our internal Data Retention Schedule, available upon request.
