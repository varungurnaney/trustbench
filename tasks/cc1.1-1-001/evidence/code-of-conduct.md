# Code of Conduct

**Document ID:** POL-COC-001
**Version:** 2.0
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2024
**Owner:** Human Resources
**Classification:** Internal
**Organization:** Meridian Healthcare Solutions

---

## 1. Purpose

This Code of Conduct establishes the ethical standards and behavioral expectations for all Meridian Healthcare Solutions employees, contractors, and board members.

## 2. Core Values

### 2.1 Integrity

We act honestly and transparently in all business dealings. We do not misrepresent facts, falsify records, or engage in deceptive practices.

### 2.2 Accountability

We take responsibility for our actions and decisions. We admit mistakes promptly and work to correct them.

### 2.3 Respect

We treat all colleagues, customers, and partners with dignity and respect regardless of position, background, or personal characteristics.

### 2.4 Excellence

We strive for excellence in our work and continuously seek to improve our skills and processes.

## 3. Professional Conduct

### 3.1 Conflicts of Interest

Employees must disclose any situation that could create a real or perceived conflict of interest. This includes:

- Financial interests in competitors, vendors, or customers
- Outside employment that could interfere with job responsibilities
- Personal relationships with vendors or customers that could influence business decisions

Disclosures should be made to the employee's manager and HR.

### 3.2 Confidentiality

Employees must protect proprietary and confidential information, including:

- Patient health information (PHI)
- Customer data and business terms
- Product roadmaps and technical architecture
- Financial information and projections
- Employee personal information

Confidential information must not be disclosed to unauthorized parties, including family members and friends.

### 3.3 Use of Company Resources

Company resources, including equipment, systems, and time, should be used primarily for business purposes. Limited personal use is acceptable if it does not interfere with work responsibilities or violate other policies.

### 3.4 Anti-Bribery and Corruption

Meridian Healthcare Solutions prohibits all forms of bribery and corruption. Employees must not offer, give, solicit, or accept bribes, kickbacks, or improper payments.

Gifts and entertainment must comply with the Gift Policy (POL-GIFT-001). Gifts exceeding $100 in value must be reported to the Compliance team.

## 4. Reporting Concerns

### 4.1 Open Door Policy

Employees are encouraged to raise concerns with their manager, HR, or the Compliance team.

### 4.2 Non-Retaliation

Meridian Healthcare Solutions prohibits retaliation against anyone who reports a concern in good faith. Retaliation will be treated as a separate policy violation.

## 5. Acknowledgment

All employees must acknowledge this Code of Conduct annually. New hires must acknowledge within their first week of employment.
