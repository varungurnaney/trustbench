## 1. Purpose

This policy defines personnel security requirements at Stratus Health Technologies to ensure workforce competency and trustworthiness in alignment with organizational objectives and regulatory requirements for healthcare technology.

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to all employees, contractors, and temporary staff who access Stratus Health Technologies information systems, including systems processing protected health information (PHI).

## 3. Pre-Employment Screening

### 3.1 Background Checks

All candidates must complete a background check through GoodHire prior to their start date. The background check includes criminal history (10-year lookback), employment verification, education verification, and professional license verification where applicable.

### 3.2 Healthcare-Specific Screening

Employees with access to PHI or clinical systems must additionally be screened against the OIG Exclusion List and GSA SAM.gov debarment list. Re-screening is conducted annually.

### 3.3 Timing

Background check results must be received and adjudicated by HR before system access is provisioned. No exceptions.

## 4. Security Awareness Training

### 4.1 Initial Training

New employees must complete security awareness and HIPAA privacy training within 5 business days of start date.

### 4.2 Annual Training

All personnel must complete annual security awareness training between January 1 and March 31 each year. Training includes both general security awareness and HIPAA-specific modules.

### 4.3 Completion Requirement

100% completion is required by March 31. Non-completers are reported to the CCO (Chief Compliance Officer) by April 7 and system access is suspended on April 15 if training remains incomplete.

## 5. Acceptable Use Policy

### 5.1 AUP Signing

All personnel must sign the AUP before receiving credentials. The AUP includes specific provisions for PHI handling.

### 5.2 Annual Re-Acknowledgment

The AUP must be re-acknowledged annually during the training cycle (January 1 - March 31).

## 6. Role Changes

### 6.1 Access Review

Role changes must trigger an access review completed by IT Security within 5 business days. PHI access changes require additional HIPAA Privacy Officer sign-off.

## 7. Termination

All access is revoked within 4 hours for involuntary terminations and 24 hours for voluntary separations.
