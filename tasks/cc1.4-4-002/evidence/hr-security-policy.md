## 1. Purpose

This policy establishes requirements for personnel security at Pinnacle Software Group to ensure all personnel are appropriately screened, trained, and managed in alignment with organizational security objectives.

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to all employees, contractors, and contingent workers who access Pinnacle Software Group information systems.

## 3. Pre-Employment Screening

### 3.1 Background Checks

All candidates for employment must complete a background check through First Advantage prior to system access provisioning. The level of screening is determined by role classification:

- **Tier 1 (High Sensitivity):** Criminal, credit, education, professional license verification. Applies to roles with production database access, financial system access, or PII handling.
- **Tier 2 (Standard):** Criminal and employment verification. Applies to all other roles.

### 3.2 Timing

Background checks must be completed before the employee's start date. If extenuating circumstances prevent completion before the start date, the CHRO may approve a temporary exception of up to 30 days with documented compensating controls.

## 4. Security Awareness Training

### 4.1 Initial Training

All new hires must complete the Wombat Security Awareness Training within their first 10 business days.

### 4.2 Annual Training

All personnel must complete annual refresher training by October 31. The training cycle opens July 1.

### 4.3 Phishing Simulation

Monthly phishing simulations are conducted by IT Security. Employees who fail two consecutive simulations must complete supplemental training within 15 business days.

### 4.4 Completion Enforcement

Personnel who do not complete annual training by October 31 are reported to the CISO. System access is suspended on November 15 if training remains incomplete.

## 5. Acceptable Use Policy

### 5.1 AUP Signing

All personnel must sign the AUP before receiving system credentials. The AUP is re-acknowledged annually as part of the training cycle.

## 6. Role Changes

### 6.1 Access Review on Role Change

When an employee's role changes, the departing manager must submit a role-change access review request in ServiceNow within 3 business days. IT Security completes the review within 5 business days.

### 6.2 Temporary Dual Access

If business continuity requires temporary retention of previous role access during a transition period, this must be approved by IT Security and limited to 30 calendar days.

## 7. Termination

All access is revoked within 24 hours.
