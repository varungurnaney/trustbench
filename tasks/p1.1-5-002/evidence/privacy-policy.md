# Meridian HR — Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** April 1, 2025
**Version:** 2.5

---

## 1. Introduction

Meridian HR Solutions Ltd ("Meridian HR," "we," "us") provides a cloud-based Human Resources Information System (HRIS) platform that enables organizations to manage employee data, payroll, benefits, performance reviews, and workforce analytics. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect personal information processed through the Meridian HR Platform (platform.meridianhr.com), our website (www.meridianhr.com), and related services.

Meridian HR is headquartered in Dublin, Ireland and is subject to the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

## 2. Information We Process

Meridian HR processes personal data as described in this policy. The categories of personal information we handle include:

**Employee Data (processed on behalf of employer clients):**
- Personal details: name, date of birth, national ID number, contact information, emergency contacts
- Employment details: job title, department, start date, employment type, manager
- Compensation: salary, bonuses, equity grants, pay history
- Benefits: health insurance selections, retirement plan enrollment, beneficiary information
- Performance: review ratings, goals, development plans, manager feedback, peer reviews
- Time and attendance: work hours, leave requests, absence records
- Training: completed courses, certifications, learning progress
- Disciplinary records: warnings, performance improvement plans, investigation notes

**Direct Contact Data (collected by Meridian HR directly):**
- Sales prospect information: name, email, company, job title
- Website visitor analytics: IP address, pages viewed, session duration
- Customer administrator contacts: name, email, phone, role

## 3. How We Use Information

We use personal information to:
- Provide the HRIS platform functionality to employer clients
- Generate workforce analytics reports shared with your employer in aggregated, anonymized form
- Improve our platform through usage analytics
- Process sales inquiries and manage customer relationships
- Send service notifications and product updates to administrators
- Comply with legal obligations

## 4. Legal Basis for Processing

- **Contractual Necessity:** Platform services delivered under contract with employer clients
- **Legitimate Interest:** Platform improvement, security, business development
- **Consent:** Where required for specific processing activities
- **Legal Obligation:** Regulatory and tax compliance

## 5. Data Sharing

We share personal information with the following service providers:

- **AWS:** Cloud hosting (eu-west-1 Dublin, eu-central-1 Frankfurt)
- **Stripe:** Payment processing for platform subscriptions
- **SendGrid:** Transactional email delivery
- **Snowflake:** Data warehousing for workforce analytics
- **HubSpot:** CRM for sales pipeline management

All providers are bound by Data Processing Agreements and process data per our instructions.

## 6. Data Retention

Employee data is retained for the duration of the employer client's contract plus 90 days for data export. Longer retention may apply where required by law. Direct contact data is retained for 24 months after last interaction.

## 7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict, port, and object to processing of your personal data. You may also withdraw consent at any time.

If you are an employee whose data is processed through the Meridian HR platform, please contact your employer's HR department to exercise your data rights.

For direct contacts and website visitors, contact privacy@meridianhr.com.
