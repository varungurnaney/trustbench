# Verdant Fintech — Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** September 1, 2025
**Version:** 2.3

---

## 1. Introduction

Verdant Fintech Inc. ("Verdant," "we," "us") provides personal finance management tools including budgeting, savings tracking, investment portfolio analysis, and financial goal planning through our mobile application and web platform (app.verdantfintech.com).

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, share, and protect your personal and financial information. Given the sensitive nature of financial data, we apply enhanced protections and transparency standards to all processing activities.

## 2. Information We Collect

**Information You Provide:**
- **Account Data:** Full name, email address, phone number, date of birth, mailing address
- **Financial Profile:** Income range, employment status, financial goals (e.g., saving for home, retirement), risk tolerance preferences
- **Bank Account Information:** Account numbers and routing numbers provided for account linking (processed through secure third-party services)
- **Investment Data:** Brokerage account details, portfolio holdings (when you choose to connect investment accounts)

**Information Collected Automatically:**
- **Device Data:** Device type, operating system, app version, device identifiers
- **Usage Data:** Features used, screens viewed, session duration, financial tools accessed
- **Location Data:** City-level geolocation for regulatory compliance (not precise GPS)

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Provide personalized budgeting, savings, and investment analysis tools
- Generate spending insights and financial recommendations
- Process account linking and data aggregation from connected financial institutions
- Send service notifications (budget alerts, goal milestones, unusual spending)
- Improve our platform and develop new financial tools
- Comply with financial regulations and anti-money laundering (AML) requirements
- Detect and prevent fraud and unauthorized access

## 4. Legal Basis for Processing

- **Consent:** Marketing communications, optional financial insights features
- **Contractual Necessity:** Core financial management services, account management
- **Legitimate Interest:** Platform improvement, fraud detection, analytics
- **Legal Obligation:** AML compliance, regulatory reporting, tax obligations

## 5. Third-Party Service Providers

We work with a limited number of trusted service providers to operate our platform. Each provider is bound by a Data Processing Agreement and processes data only as instructed by Verdant.

Our current service providers include:
- **Amazon Web Services (AWS):** Cloud hosting and data storage (us-east-1, eu-west-1)
- **Stripe Inc.:** Subscription payment processing
- **Twilio Inc.:** SMS notifications for budget alerts and two-factor authentication
- **Zendesk Inc.:** Customer support ticketing

We share only the minimum information necessary with each provider — typically limited to your email address and name for communications purposes. Payment processing is handled entirely by Stripe; we do not store credit card numbers.

We do not sell your personal or financial information to any third party.

## 6. Data Retention

We retain your personal and financial information for the duration of your active account plus 24 months after account closure. Financial transaction data is retained for 7 years to comply with IRS record-keeping requirements. Aggregated, anonymized analytics data may be retained indefinitely.

## 7. Data Security

We implement industry-leading security measures for financial data:
- AES-256 encryption at rest for all financial data
- TLS 1.3 for all data in transit
- Hardware security modules (HSM) for encryption key management
- SOC 2 Type II certified infrastructure
- Annual penetration testing by independent third parties
- Real-time fraud detection and anomaly monitoring

## 8. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal and financial data
- Correct inaccurate information
- Delete your account and associated data
- Export your data in a machine-readable format
- Opt out of marketing communications
- Opt out of the sale of personal information (California residents)

To exercise any right, contact privacy@verdantfintech.com or use the in-app privacy settings.

## 9. Children's Privacy

Verdant Fintech is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.
