## 1. Purpose

The Information Security Steering Committee (ISSC) provides executive-level oversight and strategic direction for Pinnacle Financial Group's information security program. The committee ensures that security investments, policies, and risk management practices align with business objectives and regulatory requirements.

## 2. Authority

The ISSC is authorized by the Board of Directors to:

- Approve information security policies and standards
- Review and prioritize security investments and initiatives
- Oversee risk management activities related to information security
- Escalate critical security matters to the Board when necessary
- Direct the CISO on strategic security priorities

### 3.2 Quorum

A quorum of three (3) of five (5) members must be present for the committee to conduct official business and approve decisions.

## 4. Meeting Cadence

### 4.1 Regular Meetings

The ISSC meets quarterly, with meetings scheduled in January, April, July, and October.

### 4.2 Special Meetings

The Chair or CISO may call special meetings with 48 hours notice when urgent security matters require committee attention.

## 5. Responsibilities

### 5.1 Policy Governance

- Review and approve all information security policies annually
- Ensure policies remain aligned with regulatory requirements
- Approve policy exceptions that exceed CISO authority

### 5.2 Risk Oversight

- Review the enterprise security risk register quarterly
- Approve risk treatment plans for critical and high risks
- Monitor progress on risk remediation activities

### 5.3 Compliance

- Review SOC 2 audit findings and remediation plans
- Oversee regulatory compliance activities
- Approve corrective action plans for audit findings

### 5.4 Budget and Resources

- Review and approve the annual security budget
- Prioritize security initiatives based on risk and business value
- Ensure adequate staffing for the security function

## 6. Reporting

### 6.1 Board Reporting

The ISSC Chair provides a quarterly security posture summary to the Board of Directors.

### 6.2 Meeting Minutes

Minutes are recorded by the CISO's executive assistant and distributed to all members within 5 business days of each meeting.
