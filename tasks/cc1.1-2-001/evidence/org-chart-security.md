## Key Relationships

- **CISO to CTO**: Direct reporting line. CTO approves security budget and headcount requests.
- **CISO to Board**: Quarterly briefing to Board Audit Committee (not a direct report).
- **CISO to Internal Audit**: Collaborative relationship. Internal Audit independently reviews security controls annually.
- **GRC Team to Legal**: Works closely with Privacy Officer on data protection compliance.
