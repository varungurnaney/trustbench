# Physical Security Policy

**Document ID:** POL-PS-018
**Version:** 4.0
**Effective Date:** June 1, 2025
**Owner:** Security Operations
**Classification:** Internal

---

## 1. Purpose

This policy establishes Vanguard Data Services' requirements for physical security controls to protect personnel, facilities, equipment, and information assets from unauthorized physical access, theft, damage, and environmental threats.

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to all Vanguard Data Services facilities, including the corporate headquarters at 4200 Bayshore Drive, Suite 150, Tampa, FL, and the disaster recovery office at 2800 Commerce Park, Orlando, FL.

## 3. Facility Security Zones

### 3.1 Zone Definitions

| Zone | Classification | Areas |
|------|---------------|-------|
| Public | Open | Building lobby (shared), elevator vestibule |
| General Office | Controlled | Suite 150 entrance, open floor, conference rooms, break room |
| Restricted | High Security | Server room, network closet, records vault |
| Executive | High Security | C-suite offices, board room |

### 3.2 Access Requirements

- **Public:** No Vanguard badge required
- **General Office:** Valid Vanguard employee or authorized contractor badge
- **Restricted:** Badge with Restricted authorization (Infrastructure Director approval)
- **Executive:** Badge with Executive authorization (COO approval)

## 4. Badge Management

### 4.1 Badge Types

| Type | Color | Prefix |
|------|-------|--------|
| Full-Time Employee | Blue | B- |
| Contractor | Yellow | CTR- |
| Temporary Visitor | Red | VIS- |

### 4.2 Employee Badge Issuance

Badges issued within 1 business day of start date per approved Access Authorization Form (AAF).

### 4.3 Termination Deactivation

Badge deactivation within 4 hours of HR termination notification. Physical badge collected during exit process.

### 4.4 Quarterly Review

All badge authorizations reviewed quarterly. Unused or unnecessary access revoked within 5 business days.

## 5. Surveillance

### 5.1 CCTV

Avigilon IP cameras at all entry points, server room, and parking. 24/7 recording at 4MP, 20fps.

### 5.2 Retention

CCTV footage retained for 90 days. Incident footage preserved until investigation closure.

### 5.3 Monitoring

Business hours: live monitoring at reception. After hours: motion alerts to on-call Security team.

## 6. Visitor and Vendor Management

### 6.1 Pre-Registration

All visitors must be pre-registered by the host employee via the visitor management system at least 24 hours in advance. Emergency visits require Security Manager approval.

### 6.2 Check-In

Visitors present government-issued photo ID. Identity verified against pre-registration. Temporary visitor badge issued.

### 6.3 Escort Requirement

All non-employees must be escorted by the designated host employee at all times while on premises. The host employee must maintain visual contact with the visitor. No exceptions for brief departures — if the host must leave, a substitute escort must be arranged or the visitor must wait in the reception area.

### 6.4 Zone Restrictions

Visitors are restricted to the General Office zone unless explicit written authorization for Restricted zone access is granted by the Infrastructure Director.

### 6.5 Audit and Regulatory Exception

External auditors and regulatory examiners may access Restricted zones when escorted by the Infrastructure Director or CISO for the purpose of compliance assessment or audit observation. Such visits must be pre-registered with purpose documented.

### 6.6 Vendor-Specific Controls

Vendors performing work in Restricted zones require:
- Current vendor agreement on file
- Pre-approved work order with scope of work
- Continuous host employee escort
- Tool and equipment inspection on entry and exit

### 6.7 Badge Return and Checkout

All visitor badges must be returned at reception upon departure. Departure time is logged. If a badge is not returned by end of business on the visit day, the Security Coordinator must deactivate the badge immediately and file an incident report (IR) with the Security Manager. The host employee is notified and responsible for badge recovery.

## 7. After-Hours Access

### 7.1 Business Hours

Monday through Friday, 7:00 AM to 7:00 PM Eastern Time.

### 7.2 After-Hours Authorization

Access outside business hours requires manager-approved After-Hours Access Request submitted to Security.

### 7.3 On-Call

Documented on-call rotations approved by Infrastructure Director grant automatic after-hours access. Schedules submitted monthly.
