# Human Resources Security Policy

**Version:** 5.0
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2025
**Owner:** Chief People Officer
**Classification:** Internal
**Last Reviewed:** December 10, 2024

---

## 1. Purpose

This policy establishes comprehensive personnel security requirements at Nexus Payments Inc. to ensure all individuals with access to company systems and cardholder data environments meet security competency and trustworthiness standards.

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to all employees, contractors, and third-party personnel who access Nexus Payments information systems or facilities.

## 3. Pre-Employment Screening

### 3.1 Background Checks

All candidates must complete a comprehensive background check through Accurate Background prior to system access provisioning. Checks include criminal history (7-year), employment verification (5 years), education verification, and credit check for roles with financial system access.

### 3.2 Timing

Background check results must be received and reviewed by HR before the employee's start date. No provisional access may be granted pending background check results.

## 4. Security Awareness Training

### 4.1 Initial Training

New hires must complete security awareness training within 5 business days of start date.

### 4.2 Annual Training

All personnel must complete annual security awareness refresher training. The training cycle opens January 15 and closes March 31 each year.

### 4.3 Training Content Requirements

Training content must be reviewed and updated annually by the Information Security team prior to each training cycle. Content must address:

- Current threat landscape relevant to the payment industry
- PCI DSS requirements and updates
- Social engineering and phishing techniques
- Data handling and classification
- Incident reporting procedures
- Regulatory changes affecting the organization

Content review must be documented and approved by the CISO before the training cycle opens.

### 4.4 Completion Requirement

100% completion is required by March 31. Non-completers are escalated to their VP by April 7. System access is suspended on April 15 for any employee who has not completed training.

## 5. Acceptable Use Policy

### 5.1 AUP Signing

All personnel must read and acknowledge the Acceptable Use Policy prior to receiving system credentials.

### 5.2 AUP Content

The AUP covers acceptable use of computing resources, email, internet, mobile devices, cloud services, social media, and cardholder data handling.

### 5.3 Refusal to Sign

Employees who refuse to sign the AUP may not be granted access to company information systems. The HR Business Partner and General Counsel must be consulted to determine appropriate next steps.

## 6. Role Changes

### 6.1 Access Review

All role changes trigger an automated access review. IT Security completes the review within 5 business days.

## 7. Termination

All access is revoked within 2 hours for involuntary terminations and 24 hours for voluntary separations.
