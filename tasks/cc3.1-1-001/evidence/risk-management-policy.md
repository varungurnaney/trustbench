## 1. Purpose

This policy establishes the framework for identifying, assessing, and managing information security and operational risks at Blueline Software Inc. to support SOC 2 Trust Services Criteria compliance.

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to all business units, information systems, and third-party relationships. It covers:
- Information security risks
- Technology and infrastructure risks
- Operational risks
- Regulatory and compliance risks
- Third-party and vendor risks

## 3. Risk Governance

### 3.1 Security Governance Committee (SGC)

The SGC provides oversight of the risk management program. The SGC meets quarterly to:
- Review identified risks
- Approve risk treatment plans
- Assess emerging threats

**SGC Members:**
- Diana Walsh, CISO (Chair)
- Robert Kim, VP Engineering
- Sarah Chen, General Counsel
- Michael Torres, CFO

### 3.2 Risk Owners

Each risk should have an assigned owner responsible for monitoring and reporting on the risk.

## 4. Risk Assessment

### 4.1 Annual Assessment

A risk assessment shall be conducted annually to identify and evaluate risks across all business functions. The assessment covers:
- Threat landscape analysis
- Vulnerability identification
- Impact evaluation
- Likelihood estimation

### 4.2 Risk Rating

Risks are rated using a 5x5 likelihood-impact matrix:

| Rating Range | Classification |
|-------------|----------------|
| 1-4 | Low |
| 5-9 | Medium |
| 10-15 | High |
| 16-25 | Critical |

## 5. Risk Treatment

For each identified risk, one of the following strategies is selected:
1. **Mitigate** -- Implement controls to reduce likelihood or impact
2. **Transfer** -- Shift risk via insurance or contract
3. **Accept** -- Formally accept the risk with appropriate approval
4. **Avoid** -- Eliminate the risk-creating activity

## 6. Risk Monitoring

Risks are monitored on an ongoing basis. Key risk indicators (KRIs) should be defined for significant risks.

## 7. Reporting

The CISO provides periodic updates to executive leadership on the risk posture. Critical risks are escalated immediately.

## 8. Policy Review

This policy is reviewed annually.
