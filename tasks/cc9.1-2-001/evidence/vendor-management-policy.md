# Third-Party Vendor Risk Management Policy

**Document ID:** POL-VRM-003
**Version:** 3.1
**Effective Date:** February 1, 2025
**Owner:** Security & Compliance
**Approved By:** Priya Sharma, CISO

---

## 1. Purpose

This policy establishes the framework for assessing and monitoring third-party vendor risks at Helios Analytics Inc. to ensure adequate protection of company and customer data.

## 2. Vendor Classification

| Tier | Criteria | Assessment Requirements |
|------|----------|------------------------|
| Critical | Processes customer PII, has production access, or provides core infrastructure | Annual SOC 2 Type II review, annual security questionnaire, quarterly business review |
| High | Accesses internal data or integrates with production systems | Annual SOC 2 Type II (or equivalent) review, biennial security questionnaire |
| Medium | Accesses internal systems with no customer data | Security questionnaire at onboarding, biennial review |
| Low | No system or data access | Contractual review only |

## 3. Onboarding

Before granting vendor access:
- Security questionnaire completed and reviewed
- Data Processing Agreement (DPA) executed
- SOC 2 Type II report reviewed (Critical and High tier)
- Vendor registered in Vanta
- Risk tier assigned

## 4. Ongoing Monitoring

### 4.1 SOC 2 Report Review

- Critical vendors: SOC 2 Type II report reviewed annually within 90 days of report issuance.
- High vendors: SOC 2 Type II report reviewed annually within 120 days of report issuance.
- Reports with qualified opinions or significant findings require a risk assessment and remediation tracking.

### 4.2 Business Reviews

- Critical vendors: quarterly business review covering SLA performance, incident history, and security posture.
- High vendors: semi-annual business review.

### 4.3 Security Questionnaires

- Critical vendors: annual security questionnaire.
- High vendors: biennial security questionnaire.
- Medium vendors: questionnaire at onboarding and at biennial renewal.

## 5. Contractual Requirements

All vendor contracts must include:
- Data protection and privacy obligations
- Right to audit
- Security incident notification within 72 hours
- Data return or destruction on termination
- Cyber liability insurance (Critical and High tier)

## 6. Vendor Offboarding

- Access revoked within 5 business days of termination
- Data return or destruction confirmed in writing
- Offboarding documented in Vanta
