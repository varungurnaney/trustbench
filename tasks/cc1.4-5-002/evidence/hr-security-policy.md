## 1. Purpose

This policy establishes personnel security controls at Orion SaaS Platforms to ensure workforce competency, trustworthiness, and security awareness in alignment with organizational objectives.

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to all employees, contractors, consultants, and temporary staff who access Orion SaaS Platforms information systems or facilities.

## 3. Pre-Employment Screening

### 3.1 Background Checks

All candidates must complete a background check through Sterling Check prior to system access provisioning. Requirements by role:

- **Production Access Roles:** Comprehensive check (criminal, employment, education, credit, professional references)
- **Non-Production Roles:** Standard check (criminal, employment verification)

### 3.2 Contractor Screening

Contractors must complete background checks equivalent to the employee tier for their role classification before receiving access credentials.

### 3.3 Timing

Background check results must be adjudicated before system access is granted. Provisional access is not permitted.

## 4. Security Awareness Training

### 4.1 Initial Training

New personnel must complete the CyberReady security awareness training module within 7 business days of start date.

### 4.2 Annual Training

All active personnel must complete annual security awareness training. The training window opens September 1 and closes November 30.

### 4.3 Completion Standard

The organization requires a minimum 95% completion rate by November 30 to be considered compliant. Employees who have not completed training by December 15 will have their system access suspended. Department heads are responsible for ensuring their teams achieve the 95% minimum.

### 4.4 Training Effectiveness

The Information Security team conducts quarterly phishing simulations to measure training effectiveness. A click rate exceeding 15% triggers a mandatory supplemental training session for the affected department.

## 5. Acceptable Use Policy

### 5.1 AUP Acknowledgment

All personnel must sign the AUP before receiving system credentials. The AUP is re-signed annually.

### 5.2 Refusal to Sign

If an employee or contractor refuses to sign the AUP, system access must be denied until the refusal is resolved through HR and Legal. Access may not be granted while the refusal is under review.

## 6. Role Changes

### 6.1 Access Review

Role changes trigger an automated Workday-to-Okta access review. IT Security completes reviews within 5 business days.

### 6.2 Segregation of Duties

Role changes that could create segregation of duties conflicts must be reviewed by the Compliance team before access is provisioned.

## 7. Termination

Access is revoked within 4 hours for involuntary and 24 hours for voluntary terminations.
