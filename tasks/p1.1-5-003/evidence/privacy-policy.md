# Solaris Insurance — Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** May 1, 2025
**Version:** 4.1

---

## 1. Introduction

Solaris Insurance Ltd ("Solaris," "we," "us") is a digital insurance provider offering motor, home, and health insurance products through our online platform (www.solarisinsurance.eu) and mobile applications. We are authorized and regulated by the Central Bank of Ireland.

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you obtain a quote, purchase a policy, submit a claim, or otherwise interact with our services.

## 2. Information We Collect

**Information You Provide:**
- Personal details: full name, date of birth, address, email, phone number
- Identity verification: government-issued ID, proof of address
- Insurance application data: property details, vehicle information, policy coverage requirements
- Health questionnaire: medical history, lifestyle factors (for health insurance products)
- Claims information: incident details, supporting documentation, witness statements

**Information Collected Automatically:**
- Technical data: IP address, browser type, device identifiers, operating system
- Usage data: pages viewed, quote configurations, session duration
- Location data: postcode-level location for risk assessment

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information to:
- Assess insurance applications and calculate risk using our risk assessment tools
- Provide and manage insurance policies, including renewal processing
- Process and adjudicate claims
- Detect, investigate, and prevent fraud
- Communicate policy updates, renewal notices, and service information
- Comply with insurance regulatory requirements and anti-money laundering obligations
- Improve our platform and develop new insurance products

## 4. Legal Basis for Processing

- **Contractual Necessity:** Policy administration, claims processing, premium calculation
- **Legal Obligation:** Insurance regulation, AML compliance, claims reporting
- **Legitimate Interest:** Fraud detection, platform improvement, analytics
- **Consent:** Marketing communications, optional telematics features

We process your health information as necessary to assess and manage your insurance policy.

## 5. Data Sharing

We share personal information with the following categories of service providers and partners:

- **Cloud Infrastructure:** AWS (eu-west-1 Dublin) for platform hosting and data storage
- **Payment Processing:** Stripe for premium payments and refunds
- **Reinsurance Partners:** Data shared with reinsurance counterparties as required by reinsurance treaties
- **Claims Adjusters:** Third-party loss adjusters engaged for complex claims
- **Communication:** SendGrid for transactional emails, Twilio for SMS notifications
- **Regulatory Bodies:** Data reported to the Central Bank of Ireland and Insurance Ireland as required

All service providers are bound by Data Processing Agreements.

We do not sell your personal information.

## 6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information in accordance with the following periods:
- **Active policies:** For the duration of the policy plus 12 months after expiry
- **Claims data:** 10 years after claim resolution to comply with insurance regulatory requirements
- **Quotes that did not result in a policy:** 24 months after the quote date
- **Marketing consent records:** Until consent is withdrawn plus 12 months

## 7. Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Rectify inaccurate data
- Request erasure (where not overridden by regulatory retention obligations)
- Restrict processing
- Data portability
- Object to processing
- Withdraw consent

To exercise any right, contact privacy@solarisinsurance.eu or use the privacy portal at www.solarisinsurance.eu/privacy.
