Task

You are conducting a SOC 2 Type II audit of Orion SaaS Platforms for the 2025 observation period. Review all personnel security evidence including the HR policy, training completion report, phishing simulation results, role change access review log, and HR metrics dashboard. Assess CC1.4 compliance with attention to both procedural compliance and the effectiveness of the training program. Consider whether the training program is achieving its intended outcomes. Some situations require auditor judgment about what constitutes acceptable performance versus a genuine control gap.

Evidence

  • evidence/hr-security-policy.md — Orion SaaS Platforms HR security policy defining background check tiers, training completion standards (95% minimum), phishing simulation thresholds, and role change procedures
  • evidence/training-completion-report-2025.csv — Annual security awareness training completion report for 2025 showing 20 employees with 17 completions (85%) and 3 non-completions with varying reasons
  • evidence/phishing-simulation-results-2025.csv — Quarterly phishing simulation results for 2025 showing click rates increasing from 10% to 25% across all four quarters
  • evidence/role-change-access-review-log-2025.csv — Role change access review log for 2025 showing 6 role changes with SOD reviews and proper access adjustments
  • evidence/quarterly-hr-metrics-dashboard.md — Q4 2025 HR security metrics dashboard summarizing training compliance, phishing trends, and workforce changes — provides consolidated view but contains the same data as primary evidence files [noise]
