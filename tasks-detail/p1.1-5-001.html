Task

You are a SOC 2 auditor evaluating Luminos EdTech's privacy notice practices under Trust Services Criteria P1.1. Luminos EdTech provides an online learning platform used by universities and corporate training departments. Review all provided evidence documents to assess whether the privacy notice accurately and completely describes the organization's data processing practices. This assessment requires professional judgment — several of the issues you will find involve legitimate disagreements about legal interpretation, proportionality, and materiality. For each finding, explain your reasoning about why it constitutes a genuine gap versus an acceptable business practice.
