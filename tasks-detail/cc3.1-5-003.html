Task

You are a SOC 2 auditor evaluating Cirrus Data Platforms' risk identification and assessment process for the Q4 2025 observation period (October 1 - December 31, 2025). Review the risk management policy, risk register, SGC meeting minutes, and compliance calendar. The organization has identified emerging risks through informal Slack channel discussions rather than the formal process defined in their policy. Assess whether CC3.1 requirements for risk identification and assessment are met. Consider the substance of the risk identification activities versus the documentation requirements. Some situations may require nuanced judgment.
