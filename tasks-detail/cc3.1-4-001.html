Task Description

You are a SOC 2 auditor evaluating Vertex AI Corp's risk management controls under Trust Services Criteria CC3.1. Review all provided evidence documents to assess whether the organization's risk identification, assessment, and management processes are operating effectively. Evaluate the completeness of risk coverage, timeliness of risk reviews, adequacy of risk treatment, and governance oversight. Identify any gaps or deficiencies, but be careful to distinguish genuine gaps from risks that have been properly managed through formal exception and governance processes.
Scoring: f1 · Estimated human time: 40 minutes · Documents: 5 (4 relevant)

Evidence Files

  • evidence/risk-management-policy.md (policy) — Vertex AI Corp risk management policy defining the risk assessment framework, governance structure, risk rating methodology, and treatment requirements
  • evidence/risk-register.csv (exception_register) — Active risk register with 18 risks including risk ratings, treatment plans, owners, and review dates from the ServiceNow GRC module
  • evidence/risk-assessment-report-2025.md (report) — 2025 Annual Risk Assessment Report dated March 2025 covering 15 risk categories with findings, treatment summary, and recommendations
  • evidence/exception-register.csv (exception_register) — Exception register documenting the Board-approved exception for RISK-014 (Critical-rated cross-border data transfer risk acceptance)
  • evidence/business-impact-analysis.md (documentation) — Business Impact Analysis summary identifying critical processes and recovery priorities -- related to risk management but focused on BCP/DR rather than the risk assessment process itself [noise]
