Task

You are conducting a SOC 2 Type II audit of Aegis Platform Services for Q4 2025 (October 1 - December 31, 2025). Review the maintenance policy, patching records, and exception register. Assess whether CC7.1 maintenance requirements are met. Two vendor dependency patch exceptions are documented — examine each carefully. Consider whether waiting for a vendor is always a valid reason to miss a patch SLA, and what happens when a vendor dependency is resolved but the patch is still not applied.
