Task

You are a SOC 2 auditor evaluating Crestline AI's environment segmentation controls for the Q4 2025 observation period. The organization operates an ML platform and has made architectural decisions to optimize GPU compute costs. Review the segmentation policy, VPC architecture, cost optimization review, and exception register. Assess whether CC6.6 requirements for system boundary protection and environment isolation are met. Apply professional judgment regarding whether cost optimization justifies reduced security boundaries and whether compensating controls are adequate.
