Task

Review the maintenance policy, equipment maintenance logs, and system uptime reports. Assess whether CC7.1 maintenance requirements are met. The uptime reports show 100% availability for all equipment — determine whether this is sufficient to demonstrate maintenance adequacy, or whether preventive maintenance gaps exist despite operational equipment. Consider the policy's explicit guidance on the relationship between operational status and maintenance compliance.
