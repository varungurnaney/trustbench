Task

You are conducting a SOC 2 Type II readiness assessment for Clearpoint Financial Software, a provider of portfolio management and trading analytics to institutional investors. The observation period is Q4 2025. Review the incident response policy, incident log, and customer notification log. Assess whether CC7.3 requirements for incident response — particularly regarding communication of security incidents — are met. Exercise professional judgment on notification timing decisions, especially where the policy allows investigation deferral before customer notification. Not all documents may be relevant.

  • evidence/incident-log-q4-2025.csv — Q4 2025 incident register with 7 incidents including detailed notes on customer notification decisions and investigation deferral rationale
  • evidence/customer-notification-log.csv — Customer notification decision log showing notification timing, deferral decisions, and rationale for each incident
