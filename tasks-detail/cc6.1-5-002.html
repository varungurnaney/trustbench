Task

CrestPoint is a B2B SaaS company handling enterprise customer data. Review all evidence to assess whether termination procedures are operating effectively. Several issues require professional judgment to determine materiality. Consider the overall population, the circumstances of each deviation, and whether the control is operating at a level that provides reasonable assurance.
