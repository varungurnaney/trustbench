Task

You are conducting a SOC 2 Type II readiness assessment for Prism Data Platform covering Q4 2025. Prism completed a major platform migration from VM-based to Kubernetes-native architecture during Q3-Q4 2025 and expanded into healthcare and retail customer sectors. Review all evidence and assess whether CC7.3 requirements for incident response are met. Exercise professional judgment on the adequacy of the IRP given the significant changes to the platform and business. Consider whether the annual testing frequency is sufficient given the scope of changes, and whether migration-related incidents indicate systemic issues.

Evidence

  • evidence/incident-response-policy.md — Prism Data Platform incident response policy with annual testing requirement and advisory post-major-change review provision
  • evidence/incident-log-q4-2025.csv — Q4 2025 incident register with 7 incidents including 4 directly tied to platform migration and multi-sector data incidents
  • evidence/platform-migration-summary.md — Summary of Q3/Q4 2025 major platform migration from VM-based to Kubernetes-native architecture with timeline and security review status
  • evidence/tabletop-exercise-results.md — March 2025 tabletop exercise results — conducted against pre-migration architecture with note that no post-migration exercise was performed
  • evidence/customer-onboarding-timeline.md — Customer sector expansion timeline showing 2025 entry into healthcare (HIPAA) and retail (PCI DSS) markets, with IRP coverage assessment
