Task

You are a senior SOC 2 auditor evaluating Wavecrest Financial Services' governance controls under CC1.1 (Commitment to Integrity and Ethical Values). This is a complex assessment that requires expert-level professional judgment. Review all provided evidence comprehensively. Several aspects of this organization's governance require nuanced analysis: 1. The CISO reports administratively to the CTO but functionally to an Independent Board Member. Assess whether this dual reporting structure provides adequate independence or creates ambiguity. 2. The security budget decreased 15% year-over-year. Determine whether this represents reduced governance commitment or mature operational management. 3. All metrics show strong performance. Consider whether this means governance is effective, or whether the metrics themselves need scrutiny. For each issue, provide your professional assessment with supporting reasoning. Distinguish between genuine control deficiencies and organizational design choices that may differ from textbook recommendations but are adequately compensated.

  • evidence/security-committee-charter.md — SRGC charter with dual reporting structure, independence safeguards, and executive session provisions
  • evidence/committee-meeting-minutes.md — Full SRGC meeting minutes for all four 2025 quarterly meetings including executive session summary
  • evidence/org-chart-security-function.md — Detailed security organization structure with CISO dual reporting explanation, CTO role context, and independence safeguards
  • evidence/board-security-presentation.md — Annual Board security posture presentation covering 2025 performance, budget, incidents, and governance
  • evidence/security-metrics-report.csv — Quarterly security metrics for 2025 covering incident response, vulnerability management, and awareness training
