Task

You are conducting a SOC 2 Type II audit of Prism Data Systems for Q4 2025 (October 1 - December 31, 2025). Review all evidence related to the vulnerability management program and assess CC7.1 compliance. Pay particular attention to risk acceptance practices, compensating control validation currency, and the interplay between vulnerability remediation timelines and risk acceptance durations. Not every risk acceptance with compensating controls is adequately managed — examine the details carefully.
