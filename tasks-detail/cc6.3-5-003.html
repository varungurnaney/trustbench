Task

You are a SOC 2 auditor evaluating Meridian Insights' data loss prevention and bulk export controls under CC6.3. Meridian provides business intelligence services to enterprise clients and handles large volumes of customer data. Review all evidence to assess whether the DLP controls effectively prevent unauthorized bulk data access and export. Several situations involve ambiguity about what constitutes a 'bulk export' versus normal analytical query behavior, and whether the DLP threshold is appropriately calibrated. Exercise professional judgment on each finding.
