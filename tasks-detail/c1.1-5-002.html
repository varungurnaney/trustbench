Task

You are a SOC 2 auditor evaluating Nextera Health Analytics' data protection controls under C1.1 and CC6.7. Nextera is a healthcare analytics company processing PHI under BAA agreements with healthcare providers. Review all evidence to assess encryption controls for confidential data protection. This assessment requires professional judgment on several issues: the materiality of internal network encryption gaps when compensating controls exist, the adequacy of HIPAA risk assessment findings for SOC 2 purposes, and whether identified gaps with remediation plans represent current control deficiencies or managed risks. Consider the heightened sensitivity of PHI data when making materiality assessments.

Evidence

  • evidence/data-protection-policy.md — Nextera Health Analytics data protection policy with HIPAA-specific requirements
  • evidence/tls-endpoint-audit.csv — TLS endpoint audit showing all internal and external endpoints including the de-identification service on HTTP
  • evidence/certificate-inventory.csv — Certificate inventory showing CERT-NHA-005 has no certificate for the de-identification service
  • evidence/hipaa-risk-assessment-excerpt.md — Independent HIPAA risk assessment identifying the de-identification service PHI transmission gap with compensating controls and remediation timeline
