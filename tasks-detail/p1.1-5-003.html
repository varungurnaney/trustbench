Task

You are a SOC 2 auditor evaluating Solaris Insurance's privacy notice practices under Trust Services Criteria P1.1. Solaris Insurance is an insurtech company that uses automated underwriting algorithms to assess risk and determine policy pricing. The platform processes sensitive personal data including health questionnaire responses, driving records, and financial credit data obtained from third-party data enrichment providers. Review all provided evidence documents to assess whether the privacy notice accurately and completely describes data processing practices. This assessment requires significant professional judgment regarding automated decision-making transparency, third-party data enrichment disclosure, proportionality of data collection, and the adequacy of human review mechanisms. Not all documents may be relevant.
