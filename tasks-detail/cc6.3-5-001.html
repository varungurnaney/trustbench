Task

You are a SOC 2 auditor evaluating Prism Analytics' data access and data masking controls under CC6.3. Prism handles customer PII for enterprise analytics clients. The company maintains a staging environment that receives data from production for testing purposes. Review all evidence to assess whether data masking controls are effective and whether data access in non-production environments is appropriately governed. Several issues require professional judgment about the adequacy of masking controls and whether 'test data' designations are substantive or cosmetic.

Evidence

  • evidence/data-access-policy.md — Prism Analytics data access and data masking policy covering production and non-production environments
  • evidence/data-masking-config.json — Data masking pipeline configuration showing masking rules for non-production environments
  • evidence/staging-dataset-inventory.csv — Inventory of datasets in the staging environment with source, masking status, and refresh schedule
  • evidence/access-grants-staging.csv — Staging environment database access grants showing all users and roles
