Task

You are a SOC 2 auditor evaluating NimbusCore Inc.'s vulnerability scanning program for the Q4 2025 observation period (October 1 - December 31, 2025). Review the vulnerability management policy, scan coverage report, and asset inventory. Assess whether the organization is meeting its CC7.1 obligations for vulnerability detection across all information assets. Cross-reference the coverage report against the asset inventory to identify any gaps.
