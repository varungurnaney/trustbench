Task

You are a SOC 2 auditor evaluating Cascade Analytics' quarterly access review process under CC6.1. Cascade is a data analytics company handling customer PII for enterprise clients. All Q4 2025 access reviews were completed on time and signed off. Your task is to evaluate the quality and effectiveness of the reviews, not just their completion. Cross-reference the access review results with the actual Okta user data and HR roster to determine whether the reviews achieved their intended control objective. Several issues require professional judgment about whether the reviews were genuinely effective or merely procedural.

Evidence

  • evidence/access-review-q4-detail.csv — Q4 2025 access review completion records with per-system reviewer details, timestamps, findings, and reviewer notes
  • evidence/okta-user-export.csv — Okta user export as of December 31, 2025 showing 210 accounts with roles, departments, last login dates, and account status
  • evidence/hr-roster-q4.csv — HR active employee roster as of December 31, 2025 showing 195 employees and 12 active contractors
