Task

You are a SOC 2 auditor evaluating Northgate Logistics Technology's governance controls under CC1.1 (Commitment to Integrity and Ethical Values). Review all provided evidence to assess whether governance structures and policy management practices are operating effectively. This assessment requires careful professional judgment on several points. The information security policy states reviews occur 'annually' but does not specify whether this means calendar year or rolling 12-month period. When evaluating policy review timeliness, consider the full context including committee deliberations. For CISO reporting line, assess independence in context of the organizational design. Provide nuanced assessments rather than binary pass/fail conclusions.

  • evidence/committee-meeting-minutes.csv — Information Security Committee meeting minutes for all four 2025 quarterly meetings
