Task

You are auditing Apex Cloud Solutions' SOC 2 Type II compliance for control CC8.1 (Change Management) covering Q4 2025 (October 1 - December 31, 2025). Review the change management policy, change log, and email approval threads. The organization used asynchronous email approvals for three changes when CAB meetings were cancelled. Evaluate whether the email-based approval process satisfies CC8.1 requirements and the organization's own policy. Consider the full context before making judgments -- some deviations may be reasonable operational adaptations.
