Task

You are a SOC 2 auditor evaluating Arbor Data Platforms' encryption key management controls under C1.1 and CC6.7. Review all evidence to assess whether key lifecycle controls are operating effectively. This assessment requires nuanced professional judgment on several interconnected issues: (1) Whether DB-ADP-004's 2-day tolerance overage is a material deficiency or a properly handled variance given the immediate CISO notification, exception filing, and corrective actions. (2) Whether the pattern of database key rotations consistently hitting the exact 14-day tolerance boundary indicates the control is 'operating' or 'barely operating.' (3) Whether the organization's own CISO quarterly review identifying these patterns represents strong governance or an admission of control weakness. Weigh the difference between technical compliance (within tolerance) and operational health.

Evidence

  • evidence/encryption-key-policy.md — Arbor Data Platforms encryption key lifecycle policy defining rotation schedules, tolerance windows, and escalation procedures
  • evidence/key-rotation-tracker.csv — Key rotation tracker showing all rotation events including three database keys at exactly 14-day tolerance boundary and one at 16 days
  • evidence/ciso-exception-register.csv — CISO exception register including temporary exception for DB-ADP-004 tolerance overage
  • evidence/quarterly-encryption-review-q4.md — CISO quarterly encryption review identifying the pattern of rotations consistently hitting tolerance boundaries
