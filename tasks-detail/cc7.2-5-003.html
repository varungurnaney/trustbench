Task

You are a SOC 2 auditor evaluating Aether Insurance's log management and monitoring controls for the Q4 2025 observation period. Aether Insurance is a regulated insurance company subject to state insurance regulations and SOX requirements. Review the log management policy, retention compliance audit, and daily log review records. Assess whether CC7.2 requirements for monitoring and log management are met. Consider the regulatory implications of any retention gaps and apply professional judgment to assess materiality.
