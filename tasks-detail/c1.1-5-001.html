Task

You are a SOC 2 auditor evaluating Vanguard Risk Solutions' encryption controls under C1.1 and CC6.7. Review all provided evidence to assess whether data protection controls are operating effectively. Several issues in this assessment require professional judgment to determine materiality and severity. For each finding, provide your assessment of whether the issue represents a significant control deficiency, a minor variance, or an acceptable risk given the context. Consider the difference between policy violations and actual security risk, and whether compensating factors mitigate identified gaps.

  • evidence/key-rotation-status.csv — Key rotation status log showing rotation dates, delays, and grace period compliance
