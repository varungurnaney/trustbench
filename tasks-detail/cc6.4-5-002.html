Task

Review all evidence related to their physical security program and assess CC6.4 compliance. Prism operates a corporate office and uses a third-party colocation data center. Examine badge access controls, quarterly review process, data center oversight, and visitor management. Pay close attention to dates, process completeness, and subtle gaps. Not all documents may be relevant to the assessment.
