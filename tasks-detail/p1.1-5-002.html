Task

You are a SOC 2 auditor evaluating Meridian HR's privacy notice practices under Trust Services Criteria P1.1. Meridian HR is a cloud-based HRIS platform that processes employee personal data on behalf of employer clients. The platform handles sensitive employment data including compensation, performance reviews, health benefits enrollment, and disciplinary records. Review all evidence documents and assess whether the privacy notice accurately describes data processing practices. This assessment involves several judgment calls regarding the intersection of employer-employee privacy, processor vs. controller responsibilities, and the adequacy of notice in a B2B2C context where the end data subjects (employees) may not interact directly with Meridian's privacy policy. Not all documents may be relevant to P1.1.

Evidence

  • evidence/privacy-policy.md — Meridian HR privacy policy for a cloud-based human resources information system (HRIS) platform
  • evidence/data-processing-activity-records.csv — GDPR Article 30 Record of Processing Activities listing all data flows and third-party processors
  • evidence/dsar-log.csv — Data Subject Access Request log covering Q3-Q4 2025
  • evidence/consent-management-records.csv — Consent records for optional processing activities including employee wellness and performance analytics
  • evidence/soc2-readiness-checklist.md — SOC 2 readiness checklist — general compliance checklist, not specific to P1.1 [noise]
